Shredded Pork Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Boneless Pork Shoulder Roast beef
  • 1 1/4 cupsketchup
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 1/2 cupcelery, chopped
  • 1/4 cuponion, chopped
  • 1/4 cuplemon juice
  • 3 tbspvinegar
  • 2 tbspWorcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbspbrown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tspground mustard seed
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Place roast in Dutch oven. In bowl combine all ingredients, pour over roast, cover and cook over medium low heat for 4 to 6 hours till tender. Shred meat with forks. Serve on rolls.
