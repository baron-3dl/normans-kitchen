Chicken Breasts Diane

Ingredients

  • 4 lgeboneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tspblack pepper
  • 2 tbsolive or salad oil
  • 2 tbsbutter or margarine
  • 3 tbschopped fresh chives or green onions
  • lemon juice
  • 3 tbschopped parsley
  • 2 tspDijon-style mustard
  • 1/4 cupchicken broth

Directions

  1. Place chicken breast halves between sheets of plastic wrap. Pound slightly with mallet. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat 1 tbs each of oil and butter in a large skillet.
  3. Cook chicken over high heat for 4 minutes on each side. Do not cook longer or they ill be overcooked and dry. Transfer to warm serving platter.
  4. Add chives or green onion, lime juice, parsley and mustard to pan. Cook 15 seconds, whisking constantly.
  5. Whisk in broth. Stir until sauce is smooth. Whisk in remaining butter. Serve immediately.
