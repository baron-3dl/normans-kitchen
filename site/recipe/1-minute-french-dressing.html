1-Minute French Dressing

A quick shaken French-style dressing of oil and vinegar seasoned with paprika, sugar, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tsppaprika
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1/4 cupvinegar
  • 3/4 cupoil

Directions

  1. Combine and shake well.
  1. Combine the salt, sugar, paprika, pepper, vinegar and oil, and shake well.

Goes well with

  • Balsamic Vinaigrette w/Equal
  • Mattapoisett Inn French Herb Dressing
  • Tomato Dijon Dressing
