Honey Glazed Carrots

Makes 6 to 8 servings

Photo: Thriving Vegetarian (CC BY 2.0)

Thinly sliced carrots simmered until crisp-tender, then glazed on the stovetop with honey, brown sugar, and butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbscarrots, scraped and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 3 tbsphoney
  • 3 tbsbrown sugar
  • 2 tbspbutter or margarine

Directions

  1. Combine carrots and water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 8 minutes or until crisp tender. Drain and return to pan. Add honey and remaining ingredients; cook over low heat, stirring gently, until butter and sugar melt.
  1. Combine the sliced carrots and water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 8 minutes or until crisp-tender.
  2. Drain the carrots and return them to the pan.
  3. Add the honey, brown sugar, and butter; cook over low heat, stirring gently, until the butter and sugar melt.

Goes well with

  • Marinated Carrots
  • Zesty Carrots
