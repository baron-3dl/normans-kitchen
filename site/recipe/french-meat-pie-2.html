French Meat Pie

  • 1 lbground beef
  • 1 lbground pork
  • 1 cupmashed potatoes
  • 2 tspground allspice
  • 1 tspsalt

Directions

  1. Saute onion. Remove from pan add pork and beef cook till brown. Remove fat. Combine onion, mea, spice, potatoes. Mix well. Fill pie shell, cover with top crust, brush with egg wash. Bake at 375 degrees about 30-35 minutes till crust is golden brown.
