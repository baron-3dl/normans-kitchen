Marinated Carrots

Makes 12 to 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbscarrots, peeled and sliced into 1 inch lengths
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 tspprepared mustard
  • 1/2 cupsalad oil
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupcider vinegar
  • 1 can10 3/4 oz condensed tomato soup, undiluted
  • 1 lgeonion, chopped medium fine
  • 1 lgegreen pepper chopped medium fine

Directions

  1. Bring the carrots with water to cover to a boil and boil 5 minutes; drain and cool. Into a medium bowl urn the salt, pepper and mustard; beat in a little oil to blend with the mustard; add he remaining oil and the sugar, vinegar and soup and beat again to blend. Stir in the onion and green pepper. Cover tightly and chill over night. Makes 12 to 16 servings. Recipe may be doubled. Any marinade left over may be refrigerated and used as a dressing for salad greens.
