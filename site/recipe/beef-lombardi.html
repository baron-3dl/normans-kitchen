Beef Lombardi

Ingredients

  • 1 lblean ground beef
  • 1 can14.5 oz chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can10 oz diced tomatoes w/green chilies
  • 2 tspsugar
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 can6 oz tomato paste
  • 1bay leaf
  • 1 pkg6 oz med egg noodles
  • 6green onions, chopped
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • 1 cupshredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup(4 oz) mozzarella cheese shredded

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat 5 to 6 minutes, stirring until it crumbles and is no longer pink. Drain.
  2. Stir in chopped tomatoes and next 4 ingredients; cook 5 minutes. Add tomato paste and bay leaf, and simmer 30 minutes.
  3. Cook egg noodle, according to package directions; drain.
  4. Stir together cooked egg noodles, chopped green onions, and sour cream until blended.
  5. Place noodle mixture in bottom of a lightly greased 13 by 9 inch baking dish. Top with beef mixture;sprinkle evenly with cheeses.
  6. Bake covered with aluminum foil, aa 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Uncover casserole,and bake more minutes. Garnish if desired.
  7. Can freeze this casserole up to a month. defrost in refrig overnight and bake as directed.
