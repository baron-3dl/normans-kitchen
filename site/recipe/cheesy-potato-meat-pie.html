Cheesy Potato Meat Pie

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbsground beef
  • 6 tbsfine dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tbsfinely grated onions
  • 1 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 3/4 cupevaporated milk
  • 3 cupsmashed potatoes
  • 3 tbschopped pimiento
  • 3 tbschopped green peppers
  • 1 cup(4 oz) shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, salt, pepper and evaporated milk just until blended. Press firmly into 9 inch pie plate. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes. Spoon off drippings. Combine mashed potatoes, pimiento, green pepper and 3/4 cup of the cheese. Frost meat pie with potato mixture;top with remaining 1/4 cup cheese. Bake 10 additional minutes. Makes 6 servings.
