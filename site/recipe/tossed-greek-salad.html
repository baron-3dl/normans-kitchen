Tossed Greek Salad

Makes 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 12 cupstorn romaine
  • 2 medtomatoes cut in wedges
  • 1 medcucumber, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 medgreen pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 medred onion cut into rings
  • 1/2 cupripe olives
  • 1/2 cupcrumbled feta cheese
  • Lemon Dressing
  • 1/4 cupolive oil
  • 2 tbslemon juice
  • 2 tspDijon mustard
  • 2 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 1/2 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tspdried oregano
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tspdried thyme
  • 1/8 tsppepper

Directions

  1. In a salad bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. In a jar with a tight fitting lid, combine the dressing ingredients; shake well. Pour over salad and toss to coat. Serve immediately.
