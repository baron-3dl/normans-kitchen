Chicken Romance

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 4frozen boneless skinless breasts or 8 tenderloins
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pkgfrozen broccoli florets
  • 1/3 cuplow fat milk
  • 1 pkgFrench garlic and herb Cream cheese (Boursin)
  • 4-5 thinslices ham
  • 1/2 cupItalian seasoned Parmesan bread crumbs
  • 3 tbsgrated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Prepare oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 9-10 inch square baking dish. Place frozen chicken in baking dish, season with salt and pepper and arrange broccoli evenly over top.
  2. In a small saucepan over low heat, combine cream cheese and milk, cook until smooth and creamy, stirring frequently. Pour the cream cheese mixture over broccoli; arrange ham slices evenly over top. Pour on remaining cream cheese mixture; sprinkle with bread crumbs and Parmesan. Cover and bake 40 minutes. Uncover and continue baking 20 minutes longer, or until chicken is cooked through and topping is golden brown. Serves 4
