Sweet and Sour Pork

Makes 4 servings

Photo: jeffreyw (CC BY 2.0)

A classic pork stir-fry: marinated pork loin browned with onion and green peppers, then simmered in a sweet-and-sour pineapple sauce and served over hot rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbspsherry
  • 1 tbspsoy sauce
  • 1 tbspcornstarch
  • 1 lbboneless pork loin cut in bite size pieces
  • 1/4 cupvegetable oil, divided
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 1 smallonion, sliced
  • 2green peppers, cut 1 inch
  • 1/3 cupsugar
  • 1/4 cupcatsup
  • 1 tbspsherry
  • 2 tbspsoy sauce
  • 2 tbspcornstarch
  • 1/3 cupwater
  • 1 can8 oz pineapple chunks, drained
  • Hot cooked rice

Directions

  1. Combine the first 3 ingredients; add pork, and let stand 20 minutes. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large nonstick skillet; add garlic, onion, and green pepper. Stir-fry 2 minutes or until crisp-tender; remove from skillet, and set aside.
  2. Drain pork. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in skillet; add pork. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, 3 to 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Pour off excess oil. Stir in cooked vegetables.
  3. Combine sugar and next 6 ingredients, stirring until cornstarch dissolves; add to pork mixture. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. Add pineapple, and boil 1 minute, stirring constantly. Serve over hot cooked rice.
  1. Combine the first 3 ingredients (sherry, soy sauce, and cornstarch); add the pork and let stand 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large nonstick skillet. Add the garlic, onion, and green pepper and stir-fry 2 minutes or until crisp-tender; remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Drain the pork. Heat the remaining 2 tablespoons oil in the skillet; add the pork. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, 3 to 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Pour off the excess oil, then stir in the cooked vegetables.
  4. Combine the sugar, catsup, sherry, soy sauce, cornstarch, and water, stirring until the cornstarch dissolves; add to the pork mixture. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil.
  5. Add the pineapple and boil 1 minute, stirring constantly. Serve over hot cooked rice.

  • Infallible Rice
