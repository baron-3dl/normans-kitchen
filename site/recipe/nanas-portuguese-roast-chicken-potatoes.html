Nana's Portuguese Roast Chicken & Potatoes

A Portuguese-style oven roast of Idaho potatoes with chorizo, onion, tomato, and parsley in a seasoned red-wine-vinegar and water bath that gives the potatoes a red color.

Ingredients

  • 3 cupswater
  • 1or 2 tbsgood red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbspaprika
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1 tbsfresh ground pepper
  • 1 medonion chopped
  • 1tomato, chopped
  • 3 tbsfresh chopped parsley
  • 4Idaho potatoes
  • 1Chorizo (Portuguese sausage)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Pour water into roasting pan. Add vinegar to taste. The water should have just a hint of vinegary taste. Sprinkle paprika over the water and mix, removing the lumps. Add salt, pepper, onion, tomato, parsley and potatoes. Before cooking add chorizo. Bake for 2 hours. Do not cover potatoes because they will steam and not roast. The potatoes will have a nice red color when they are done.
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Pour the 3 cups water into a roasting pan. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar to taste; the water should have just a hint of a vinegary taste.
  3. Sprinkle the 1 tablespoon paprika over the water and mix, removing the lumps.
  4. Add the salt, pepper, chopped onion, chopped tomato, chopped parsley, and the potatoes.
  5. Before cooking, add the chorizo.
  6. Bake for 2 hours. Do not cover the potatoes, because they will steam and not roast.
  7. The potatoes will have a nice red color when they are done.
