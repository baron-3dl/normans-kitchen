Tomato Dijon Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cupplain yogurt
  • 1/2 cupketchup
  • 1 bsDijon mustard
  • 1 tbsred wine vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Stir together all ingredients and chill overnight in a covered container.
  2. HINT: For a more herbal finish, substitute tarragon vinegar and add 1/4 tsp dried tarragon.
  3. Adding 1/4 cup of relish of your choice makes this a low calorie Russian style dressing.
