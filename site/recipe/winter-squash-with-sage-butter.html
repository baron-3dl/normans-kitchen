Winter Squash with Sage Butter

Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lbswinter squash (butternut, acorn, Hubbard)
  • 2 tbsolive oil
  • 1 1/2 tspsalt, divided
  • 1/2 tspground pepper
  • 3 tbsbutter
  • 1/2 cupshallots
  • 2 tbschopped fresh sage (or 1 tsp dried)

Directions

  1. Arrange oven rack in lower third of oven. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Line jellyroll pan with foil; set aside.
  2. Cut squash into 2 inch thick pieces, discarding seeds; transfer to bowl. Add oil, 1/2 tsp salt and the pepper; toss. Arrange squash skin side down on prepared pan. Bake 30 minutes. Turn pieces over and bake until squash cab easily be pierced with a knife, 15 minutes more. Let stand until cook enough to handle. remove skin with small paring knife; transfer squash to bowl and gently stir with spoon until coarsely mashed.
  3. Melt butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Add shallots; cook until they soften and just begin to color, 5 minutes. Stir in sage and cook 2 minutes more. Add sage-butter ad remaining 1 tsp salt to squash. Serves 8.
