Baked Flounder or Sole

Flounder or sole fillets baked over a bed of sauteed onions and cooked carrots, covered with a sour cream and tomato soup sauce and topped with potato chips.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupsour cream
  • 1/2 cantomato soup
  • 1 lbflounder, or fillet of sole
  • 1onion chopped
  • FewCarrots, cooked, sliced

Directions

  1. Saute onions. Place carrots and onions on bottom of baking dish. Place fish on top. In a bowl mix sour cream and tomato soup for sauce. Pour sauce over fish. Top with potato chips. Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes until done.
