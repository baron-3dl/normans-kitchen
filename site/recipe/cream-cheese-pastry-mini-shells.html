Cream Cheese Pastry Mini Shells

A simple three-ingredient pastry made from flour, cream cheese, and butter, pressed into miniature muffin pans and baked into small shells that can be filled with fruit or almost any filling.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cupsflour
  • 1 pkg8 oz. cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 1/4 cupsbutter or margarine (softened)

Directions

  1. Mix and stir all ingredients till well blended. Make into 3/4" balls. Press into miniature muffin pans to form shells. Bake at 400 degrees 10-12 minutes. Cool on racks. Fill with almost any filling, fruit, etc.
  1. Mix and stir all ingredients (flour, cream cheese, and butter or margarine) until well blended.
  2. Make the dough into 3/4-inch balls.
  3. Press each ball into miniature muffin pans to form shells.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
  5. Cool the shells on racks.
  6. Fill with almost any filling, fruit, etc.

Goes well with

  • No Roll Pastry Shell
  • Fresh Fruit Tarts
  • Ricotta Cheese Filling
  • Perfect Pie Crust
