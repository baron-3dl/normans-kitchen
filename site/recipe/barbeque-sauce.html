Barbeque Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupWesson oil
  • 3/4 cupchopped onions
  • 3/4 cupketchup
  • 3/4 cupwater
  • 1/3 cuplemon juice
  • 3 bsugar
  • 3 tbsWorcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsmustard
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Cook onion until soft in hot oil. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer for 1 minutes.
