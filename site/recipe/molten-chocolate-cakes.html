Molten Chocolate Cakes

Photo: barisione (CC BY 2.0)

Individual semi-sweet chocolate cakes baked in custard cups until the sides are firm but the centers stay soft and molten, then inverted and served with whipped topping.

Ingredients

  • 4 squaresBakers semi sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cupbutter
  • 1 cuppowdered sugar
  • 2eggs
  • 2egg yolks
  • 6 tbspflour
  • 1/2 cupthawed cool whip topping

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Butter 4 (3/4 cup) custard dishes. Place on baking sheet.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter over a double boiler or microwave Stir with wire whisk until all is melted. Stir in sugar until well blended. Whisk in eggs and egg yolks. Stir in flour. Divide batter evenly to all custard cups.
  3. Bake 13 to 14 minutes or until sides are firm but centers are soft. Let stand 1 minute. Carefully run knife around cakes to loosen. Invert cakes to dessert dishes. Serve with whipped cream topping.
  4. Batter can be made a day ahead. Pour into prepared custard cups. Cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate. When ready. Bake as directed.

  8. Serve with the thawed Cool Whip whipped topping.
