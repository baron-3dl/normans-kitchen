Veal Cutlets

Ingredients

  • 1 lb.veal cutlets
  • 1 Tbsplemon juice
  • 1Egg
  • 1 tbspwater
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1/2 Cupbread crumbs
  • 1/4 cupparmesan cheese grated lemon peel
  • 1/4 cupflour
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. Slice veal into thin slices and pound with mallet to 1/4" thickness. Combine egg, water, oil, beat well. Combine bread crumbs and spices. Dip cutlets in egg mixture and then crumb mixture. Then fry in margarine. Sprinkle with grated parmesan.
