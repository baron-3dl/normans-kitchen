Veal Cutlets

Photo: Augapfel (CC BY 2.0)

Thin veal cutlets pounded flat, coated in a Parmesan bread-crumb mixture, and pan-fried in margarine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb.veal cutlets
  • 1 Tbsplemon juice
  • 1Egg
  • 1 tbspwater
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1/2 Cupbread crumbs
  • 1/4 cupparmesan cheese grated lemon peel
  • 1/4 cupflour
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. Slice veal into thin slices and pound with mallet to 1/4" thickness. Combine egg, water, oil, beat well. Combine bread crumbs and spices. Dip cutlets in egg mixture and then crumb mixture. Then fry in margarine. Sprinkle with grated parmesan.
  1. Slice the veal into thin slices and pound with a mallet to 1/4-inch thickness.
  2. In a bowl, combine the egg, water, and oil; beat well.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the bread crumbs and the spices.
  4. Dip the cutlets in the egg mixture, then in the crumb mixture.
  5. Fry in the margarine.
  6. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

Goes well with

  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Veal (or Chicken) Spaghetti
