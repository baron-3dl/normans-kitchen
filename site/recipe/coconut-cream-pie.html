Coconut Cream Pie

Photo: Accretion Disc (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 1/4 cupcornstarch
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 2 cupsmilk
  • 3egg yolks
  • 2 tbspmargarine
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 cupcoconut flaked
  • 1 bakedPie shell
  • 1 cupwhipping cream
  • 1/4 cupsifted powdered sugar
  • 2 tbspcoconut toasted

Directions

  1. Combine 3/4 cup sugar, cornstarch, salt in sauce pan, gradually stir in milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly till thickened/bubbly. Cook 1 minute. Beat egg yolks, gradually stir about 1/4 of hot mixture into yolks, add to remaining hot mixture, stirring always. Cook 30 seconds. Remove from heat stir in butter, vanilla and 1 cup coconut. Pour into shell. Cool completely. Cover and chill 1-2 hours.
  2. Beat whip cream on high speed till foamy. Add powder sugar, beat till peaks form. Pipe or decorate with cream, add toasted coconut.
