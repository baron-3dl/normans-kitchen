Snowballs

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg6 oz semi sweet tid bits
  • 1/3 cupevaporated milk
  • 1 cupsifted confectioners sugar
  • 1/2 cupchopped walnuts
  • 1 can3 1/2 oz flaked coconut or ground nuts

Directions

  1. Combine chocolate and milk in double boiler, heat over hot water until chocolate is melted, stirring to blend. Remove from heat, stir in confectioners sugar and nuts. Cool slightly then form into 1 inch balls roll into coconut or nuts.
