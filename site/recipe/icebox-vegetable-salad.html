Icebox Vegetable salad

Serves 6-8

Ingredients

  • 1 can16 oz. green beans, drained
  • 1 can17 oz tiny peas, drained
  • 1 can16 oz. whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 jar4 oz chopped pimiento, drained
  • 1 cupfinely chopped celery
  • 1 medonion, finely chopped
  • 1 medgreen pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cupvinegar
  • 1/2 cupvegetable oil
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Combine all vegetables in a large glass bowl; set aside. In a saucepan, combine sugar, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper; bring to a boil. Cool slightly and pour over vegetables. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Serves 6-8
