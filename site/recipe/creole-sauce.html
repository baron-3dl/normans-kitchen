Creole Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cupsfinely chopped onions
  • 1 cupfinely chopped celery
  • 2 medpeppers finely chopped
  • 2 clovesgarlic pressed
  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • 1 can (15 oz)tomato sauce
  • 1 cupwater
  • 2 tspminced parsley
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tspcayenne pepper
  • 1bay leaves

Directions

  1. Cook and stir onion, celery, pepper and garlic in butter for 5 min or til tender. Remove from heat and add tomato sauce, water and spices. Simmer 10-15 minutes til sauce is of consistency you want.
