Zucchini Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 stripsbacon chopped
  • 2 cupssmall diced onions
  • 5 clovesgarlic, sliced
  • 8 cupsZucchini, cut in half moons 1/2 inch thick
  • 5-6 2 cupsplum tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cupprepared tomato sauce
  • 4 tbsfresh chopped oregano
  • 3 cupsshredded mozzarella
  • 1 cupplain bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cupgrated Parmesan
  • 1 tbsolive oil

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Place bacon and onion in 6-8 quart pot on medium heat. Cook until light brown. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  3. Toss in zucchini and cook 2-3 minutes. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook 2 minutes. Add oregano and tomato sauce and remove from heat.
  4. Line bottom of 2 quart casserole dish with light layer of shredded mozzarella. Top cheese with half of zucchini mixture. Place more cheese on top and add rest of zucchini mix. Top with remaining mozzarella, bread crumbs and Parmesan. Drizzle 1 tablespoon of oil on top of cheese and bread crumbs.
  5. Bake uncovered 1 1/2 hours or until hot and bubbly.
