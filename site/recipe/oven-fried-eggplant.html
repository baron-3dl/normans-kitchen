Oven Fried Eggplant

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupbreadcrumbs
  • 2 tbspParmesan (grated)
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 medeggplant
  • 1/4 cupmayonnaise or salad dressing
  • Basil for Garnish

Directions

  1. Combine first four ingredients in shallow dish. Peel eggplant and cut into 1/4 inch slices. Spread both sides with mayo/dressing, dredge in crumbs, place on cookie sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 10-12 minutes til golden brown.
