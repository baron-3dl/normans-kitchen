Oven Fried Eggplant

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Eggplant slices coated in seasoned Parmesan breadcrumbs and oven-baked until golden brown, a lighter alternative to pan-frying.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupbreadcrumbs
  • 2 tbspParmesan (grated)
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 medeggplant
  • 1/4 cupmayonnaise or salad dressing
  • Basil for Garnish

Directions

  1. Combine first four ingredients in shallow dish. Peel eggplant and cut into 1/4 inch slices. Spread both sides with mayo/dressing, dredge in crumbs, place on cookie sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 10-12 minutes til golden brown.
  1. Combine the first four ingredients (breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper) in a shallow dish.
  2. Peel the eggplant and cut it into 1/4 inch slices.
  3. Spread both sides of each slice with mayonnaise or salad dressing, then dredge in the crumb mixture.
  4. Place the slices on a cookie sheet.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 10-12 minutes, until golden brown.
  6. Garnish with basil.

Goes well with

  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Oven Fried Potatoes
