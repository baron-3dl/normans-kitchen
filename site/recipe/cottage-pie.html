Cottage Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 lgechopped onions
  • 2 tbsbutter
  • 2 tbsflour
  • 1/2 pintstock
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 lbcooked ground meat
  • 1 lbpotatoes
  • margarine and milk

Directions

  1. Fry the chopped onion in butte, slowly stir in the flour. Add stock gradually until the mixture thickens. Add seasonings. Add meat. Place mixture in a well greased pie dish. Boil potatoes, and mash with margarine and milk. Spread over the meat mixture. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
