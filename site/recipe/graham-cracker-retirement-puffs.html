Graham Cracker Retirement Puffs

Cream-puff shells made from a graham cracker choux-style paste, baked until puffed, then split and filled with a creamy vanilla filling.

Ingredients

  • 8 sqgraham crackers (crumbs 1/2 cup)
  • 3 tbsflour
  • 2/3 cupwater
  • 1/3 cupbutter
  • 1/8 tspsalt
  • 2eggs

Directions

  1. Combine crumbs and flour, set aside. In saucepan over high heat, heat water, butter and salt until butter melts and mixture begins to boil. Quickly stir in crumb mixture beating vigorously until mixture leaves sides of pan in a ball. Add eggs one at a time beating until blended. Chill pan in ice water until cool. Drop batter on sheet in 6 mounds, swirling top of each.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Bake for 40 min. Turn off oven. With sharp knife make a small slit in each puff.
  3. Cool. Cut off top of puffs with sharp knife.
  4. Fill with your favorite creamy vanilla filling. I use cool whip and pudding.
