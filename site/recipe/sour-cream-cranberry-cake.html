Sour Cream Cranberry Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupmargarine
  • 3 wholeeggs
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 2 cupschopped fresh cranberries
  • brown sugar and cinnamon to taste

Directions

  1. Cream margarine and sugar. Add eggs one at a time. Sift dry ingredients together and add to creamed mixture with sour cream ending with flour mixture. Stir in vanilla. old in one cup of cranberries. Pour half of the batter into greased 10 inch tube pan, cover with remaining batter. Sprinkle lightly with brown sugar and cinnamon. Bake at 325 degrees for one hour.
