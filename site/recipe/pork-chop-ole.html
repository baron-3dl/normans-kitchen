Pork Chop Ole'

Makes 4-6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6pork chops 1/2 in thick
  • 2 1/2 tbscooking oil
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cupuncooked long grain rice
  • 1 1/2 cupswater
  • 1 can8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1/2 envTaco Seasoning Mix (2 tbsp)
  • 1 medgreen pepper, chopped
  • 3/4 cupshredded cheese

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, brown pork chops in oil. Sprinkled with seasoned salt and pepper. Meanwhile in a greased 13 x 9 x 3 baking dish, combine rice, water, tomato sauce and taco seasoning, mix well. Arrange pork shops over rice, top with green pepper. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Uncover and sprinkle with cheese. Return to oven until cheese is melted.
