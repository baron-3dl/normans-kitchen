Boston Cream Pie Loaf

A quick no-bake dessert made from a sliced frozen pound cake layered with vanilla pudding and topped with chocolate ice cream topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 16 ozfrozen pound cake, thawed
  • 2 cupsrefrigerated low fat vanilla pudding
  • 1/2 cupchocolate ice cream topping

Directions

  1. Slice pound cake into 3 layers using a long serrated knife. Place bottom layer on serving dish.
  2. Spread evenly with 1 cup pudding. Top with middle layer of cake; spread with remaining pudding.
  3. Top with remaining cake layer, cut side down. Spread with chocolate topping, pushing it gently over edges so topping drips down sides of cake. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 3 days.
