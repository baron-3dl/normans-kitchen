Country French Chicken Breasts

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsbutter or margarine
  • 1 lbboneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1 envLipton Onion soup mix w/Garlic and Herbs
  • 1 cupwater
  • 1 tbslemon juice
  • 4lemon slices
  • Hot cooked rice

Directions

  1. In 12-inch skillets, melt butter over medium high heat anbd brown chicken. Stir in savory herb sauce with garlic soup mix blended with waater and lemon juice; arrange lemon slices on chicken. Reduce heat to low and simmoer covered 10 minutes or until sauce ois slightly thickened and chicken is done. To serve, arrange chicken over rice and spoon sauce over chicken. Makes about 4 servings.
