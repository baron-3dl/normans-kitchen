Too Good Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 8 ozfat free cream cheese
  • 3/4 cupskim ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cupfirmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 tspvanilla extract

Directions

  1. Process all ingredients in food processor until well blended. Frost a 13 x 9 in apple, banana, or spice cake
