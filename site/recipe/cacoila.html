Cacoila

Cacoila is a Portuguese-style slow-cooked seasoned pork, made with pork butt or shoulder simmered all day with beer, garlic, paprika, and crushed red pepper.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lbspork butt or pork shoulder (cut in chunks)
  • paprika
  • crushed red pepper seeds
  • salt and pepper
  • garlic
  • can of beer

Directions

  1. Cook in slow pot all day on low.
  1. Place the pork butt or pork shoulder chunks in a slow cooker (slow pot).
  2. Add the paprika, crushed red pepper seeds, salt and pepper, garlic, and the can of beer.
  3. Cook on low all day.

Goes well with

  • Portuguese Pork Chops
  • Pulled Pork
  • Chourico Stewed w/Cabbage
