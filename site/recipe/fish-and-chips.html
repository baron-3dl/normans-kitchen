Fish and Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbsfillets
  • 1 cupflour
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • Batter
  • 1 cupmilk
  • 3eggs, beaten
  • 1 cupflour
  • 1/4 tspsalt

Directions

  1. Rinse and dry fish. Roll in flour seasoned with salt. Dip into batter, coating well. Heat oil to 375 degrees in saucepan, fry fish 4 minutes or until golden brown. Drain. Keep hot in low oven. Add potatoes to fat and fry as usual.
