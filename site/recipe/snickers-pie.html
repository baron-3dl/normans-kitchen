Snickers Pie

A frozen peanut butter and chocolate pie made by blending frozen yogurt or ice cream with dry chocolate pudding mix, whipped topping, chunky peanut butter, and Grape Nuts cereal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cupfrozen yogurt or ice cream
  • 1 pkginstant chocolate pudding mix
  • 8 ozcool whip
  • 1/3 cupchunky peanut butter
  • 1/3 cupgrape nuts cereal

Directions

  1. Mix all together (pudding is dry) Spread into pie plate and freeze.
  1. Mix all of the ingredients together (the chocolate pudding mix goes in dry).
  2. Spread into a pie plate and freeze.

Goes well with

  • Peanut Butter Pie
  • Grape Nut Pudding
  • Peanut Butter Dessert
