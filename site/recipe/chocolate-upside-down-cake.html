Chocolate Upside Down Cake

Ingredients

  • 3/4 darkcorn syrup
  • 1/3 cuplight brown sugar
  • 1/4 cupbutter or margarine
  • 1/8 tspsalt
  • 2/3 cuppecans
  • 1 pkgfudge cake mix
  • 1egg
  • 1/2 cupChocolate Tid Bits
  • Whipped Cream

Directions

  1. In a saucepan combine syrup and sugar, margarine and salt. Bring to a boil stirring until butter is melted. Stir in nuts. Pour into 9 x 9 in pan.
  2. Mix cake mix as directed. Sprinkle tid bits over mixture in pan. Pour batter over tid bits. Bake 35-40 minutes.
