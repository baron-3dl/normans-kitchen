Five Spice Powder Blend

Makes 1/4 cup

Ingredients

  • 2 tspanise seeds, crushed
  • 2 tspfreshly ground pepper
  • 2 tspfennel seeds, crushed
  • 2 tspground cloves
  • 2 tspground ginger
  • 2 tspground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tspground allspice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients; store in an airtight container. Use to flavor fish or pork.
