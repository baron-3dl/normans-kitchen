Five Spice Powder Blend

Makes 1/4 cup

A homemade five-spice mix of anise, fennel, cloves, ginger, and cinnamon for flavoring fish or pork.

Ingredients

  • 2 tspanise seeds, crushed
  • 2 tspfreshly ground pepper
  • 2 tspfennel seeds, crushed
  • 2 tspground cloves
  • 2 tspground ginger
  • 2 tspground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tspground allspice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients; store in an airtight container. Use to flavor fish or pork.
  1. Combine all ingredients: the crushed anise seeds, freshly ground pepper, crushed fennel seeds, ground cloves, ground ginger, ground cinnamon, and ground allspice.
  2. Store in an airtight container. Use to flavor fish or pork.

Goes well with

  • Creole Seasoning Blend
  • Emeril's Essence
  • Sweet and Sour Pork
