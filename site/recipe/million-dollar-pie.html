Million Dollar Pie

Ingredients

  • 2Baked Pie Crusts
  • 1 cancondensed milk
  • 1/4 cuplemon juice
  • 1 pkgfroze strawberries 10 oz
  • 1 smcan crushed pineapple
  • 1 9 ozcool whip

Directions

  1. Mix all together and put into pie crust. Set overnight in refrigerator.
