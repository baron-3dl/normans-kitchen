Golden Squash Bake

Ingredients

  • 3 lbsyellow squash
  • 2 lgeonions, chopped
  • 1 tbminced garlic
  • Coarse salt, ground pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cupolive oil
  • 3/4 cupslivered fresh basil leaves

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cut the squash into 1 inch cubes and place in a large bowl, Toss with the onions,garlic, coarse salt and pepper and 1/4 cup olive oil
  3. Place the vegetables in a shallow baking pan to fit and bake until tender, tossing once or twice, about 1 1/4 hours. Transfer the mixture to a bowl, season generously with more salt and pepper, and drizzle with extra olive oil, if you like. toss with basil and serve immediately. Serve 4 to 6
