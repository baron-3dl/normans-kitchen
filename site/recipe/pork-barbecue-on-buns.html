Pork Barbecue on Buns

Photo: OakleyOriginals (CC BY 2.0)

Boneless pork loin slow-cooked with cola, onion, and barbecue sauce until tender, then sliced or shredded and served on sandwich buns.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs.boneless pork loin
  • 3/4 cupcola carbonated beverage
  • 1onion, chopped
  • 3/4 cupbarbecue sauce
  • 8sandwich buns

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except buns in 4 quart slow cooker; cook, covered on high for 5-6 hours, until very tender. Drain and slice or shred pork; serve on buns with additional sauce. if desired.
  1. Combine all ingredients except the buns in a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 5 to 6 hours, until the pork is very tender.
  3. Drain and slice or shred the pork.
  4. Serve on the buns, with additional barbecue sauce if desired.

Goes well with

  • Shredded Pork Sandwiches
  • Shredded Pork Sandwiches
  • Pulled Pork
