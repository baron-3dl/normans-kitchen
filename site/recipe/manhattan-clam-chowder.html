Manhattan Clam Chowder

Makes 4-6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 largepotatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 slicesbacon diced
  • 2 smallonions chopped
  • 1green pepper finely diced
  • 1 stalkscelery, diced
  • 1 quartboiling water
  • 2 tbsptomato paste
  • 1 can (16 0z.)tomatoes chopped
  • 1 can 10 0z)minced clams
  • 1/2 tspthyme
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cube bacon and brown in soup pan. Add potatoes and onions turn to glaze. Add green pepper and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, to glaze. Add boiling water, tomatoes and paste, liquid drained from clams and thyme. Simmer about 5 minutes, until potatoes are tender. Add clams, heat briefly, season with salt and pepper. Makes 4-6 servings.
