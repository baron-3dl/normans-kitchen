Zesty Carrots

Photo: PattayaPatrol (CC BY-SA 4.0)

Crisp-tender julienned carrots baked under a tangy mayonnaise-horseradish sauce with a buttered breadcrumb and paprika topping.

Ingredients

  • 7 medcarrots jullienned
  • 1/2 cupmayonnaise
  • 2 tbsphorseradish sauce
  • 2 tbspgrated onions
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1/4 cupfine breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbspmargarine melted
  • paprika

Directions

  1. Cook carrots 5 minutes until crisp tender. Put carrots in casserole dish. Combine next 5 ingredients. Spread over carrots. Mix butter and crumbs, spread over carrots, sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 375 degrees 15 minutes.
  1. Cook the carrots for 5 minutes, until crisp-tender.
  2. Put the carrots in a casserole dish.
  3. Combine the mayonnaise, horseradish sauce, grated onions, salt, and pepper. Spread over the carrots.
  4. Mix the melted margarine and breadcrumbs; spread over the carrots. Sprinkle with paprika.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Marinated Carrots
  • Honey Glazed Carrots
