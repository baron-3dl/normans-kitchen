Pumpkin Chiffon Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cupspumpkin
  • 3egg yolks, slightly beaten
  • 3/4 cupdark Karo
  • 2/3 cupmilk
  • 1/2 tspeach ginger, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt
  • 1 envplain gelatin
  • 1/4 cupcold water
  • 3egg whites stiffly beaten
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • baked pastry shell
  • 1/2 cupwhipped cream

Directions

  1. Place pumpkin in top of double boiler, add beaten egg yolks, karo, milk,spices and salt. Cook over boiling water until custard consistency,stirring constantly. Remove from heat. To hot custard add gelatin which has been softened in cold water. Stir until gelatin is dissolved. Cool. When mixture begins to thicken, ea egg whites until stiff, beat in sugar. Gently fold into slightly thickened pumpkin mixture. Pour into shell and chill until firm. Decorate with whipped cream.
