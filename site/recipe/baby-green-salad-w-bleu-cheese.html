Baby Green Salad w/Bleu Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 5 ozbag baby greens
  • 1 lgehead iceberg lettuce, cut in bite size pcs
  • 1 lgeromaine heart, cut in bite size pcs
  • 3/4 cuptoasted pecans, coarsely chopped
  • 3/4 cupdried cranberries
  • 1 cupcrumbled bleu cheese
  • 1/2 cupolive oil
  • Coarse salt and ground pepper
  • 2 tbsaged balsamic vinegar

Directions

  1. Place greens in large bowl. Add nuts, berries and cheese. Drizzle oil over top. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss to coat.
  2. Taste for seasonings and adjust as needed.
  3. Drizzle vinegar over salad and toss to coat again.
