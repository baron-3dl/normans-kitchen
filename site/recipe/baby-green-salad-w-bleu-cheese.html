Baby Green Salad w/Bleu Cheese

A mixed green salad of baby greens, iceberg and romaine with toasted pecans, dried cranberries and bleu cheese, dressed simply with olive oil and aged balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 5 ozbag baby greens
  • 1 lgehead iceberg lettuce, cut in bite size pcs
  • 1 lgeromaine heart, cut in bite size pcs
  • 3/4 cuptoasted pecans, coarsely chopped
  • 3/4 cupdried cranberries
  • 1 cupcrumbled bleu cheese
  • 1/2 cupolive oil
  • Coarse salt and ground pepper
  • 2 tbsaged balsamic vinegar

Directions

  1. Place greens in large bowl. Add nuts, berries and cheese. Drizzle oil over top. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss to coat.
  2. Taste for seasonings and adjust as needed.
  3. Drizzle vinegar over salad and toss to coat again.
  1. Place the baby greens, iceberg lettuce and romaine heart in a large bowl. Add the pecans, cranberries and bleu cheese. Drizzle the olive oil over the top. Season with coarse salt and ground pepper to taste. Toss to coat.
  2. Taste for seasonings and adjust as needed.
  3. Drizzle the balsamic vinegar over the salad and toss to coat again.

Goes well with

  • Apple and Spinach Salad
  • Balsamic Vinaigrette w/Equal
  • Tossed Greek Salad
