Devil Dog Filling

A creamy filling made by beating together evaporated milk, shortening, sugar, salt, and vanilla.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cupevaporated milk
  • 2/3 cupshortening
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together, beat for about 5 minutes until creamy.
