Honey Glazed Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs.Chicken Wings
  • 1 cuphoney
  • 1/2 cupsoy sauce
  • 2 tbspveg oil
  • 2 tbspfresh gingerroot
  • 2 tbspcatsup
  • 1 clovemince garlic
  • 2 tbspmixed scallions

Directions

  1. Make sauce with honey and spices. Marinate wings in sauce in a baking dish in refrigerator for hour or more, turning now and then. Lightly grease cookie sheet and place wings on and bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes turning to brown all over.
