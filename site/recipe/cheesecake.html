Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 lbricotta
  • 1 lbsour cream
  • 1 lbcream cheese, (2, 8 oz pkg)
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 1 stickbutter or margarine,melted
  • 3 lgeeggs
  • 3 tbsflour tortillas
  • 3 tbscorn starch
  • 4 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 4 1/2 tsplemon juice

Directions

  1. Place ingredients in order given in a large mixing bowl. Beat with an electric mixer. Start on low speed move to medium and finish on high. Approximately 10 minutes total.
  2. Batter should be smooth and liquid. Grease and flour a 10 inch round spring form pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour batter into pan and bake 1 hour. turn oven off and leave cake in oven for another hour. Cool and refrigerate.
