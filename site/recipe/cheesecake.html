Cheesecake

Photo: ginnerobot (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A baked cheesecake made with ricotta, sour cream, and cream cheese in a springform pan, then cooled slowly in the turned-off oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbricotta
  • 1 lbsour cream
  • 1 lbcream cheese, (2, 8 oz pkg)
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 1 stickbutter or margarine,melted
  • 3 lgeeggs
  • 3 tbsflour tortillas
  • 3 tbscorn starch
  • 4 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 4 1/2 tsplemon juice

Directions

  1. Place ingredients in order given in a large mixing bowl. Beat with an electric mixer. Start on low speed move to medium and finish on high. Approximately 10 minutes total.
  2. Batter should be smooth and liquid. Grease and flour a 10 inch round spring form pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour batter into pan and bake 1 hour. turn oven off and leave cake in oven for another hour. Cool and refrigerate.
  1. Place all ingredients, in the order given, in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Beat with an electric mixer, starting on low speed, moving to medium, and finishing on high, for approximately 10 minutes total. The batter should be smooth and liquid.
  3. Grease and flour a 10-inch round springform pan.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 1 hour.
  5. Turn the oven off and leave the cake in the oven for another hour.
  6. Cool and refrigerate.
