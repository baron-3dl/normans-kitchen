R. C. Shea's House Dressing

A creamy poppy seed house dressing made from oil, wine vinegar, and mayonnaise, seasoned with onion, ketchup, mustard, sugar, and a touch of cayenne. Makes about 2 cups.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cupolive oil or veg oil
  • 1/4 cupwine vinegar
  • 1 bslemon juice
  • 2 tbsminced onions
  • 1 tbsketchup
  • 1/2 tspdried mustard
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 tbssugar
  • 2 dashescayenne pepper
  • 1 cupmayonnaise
  • 2 tbspoppy seeds

Directions

  1. Place oil, vinegar, lemon juice, onion, ketchup, mustard, salt, sugar and cayenne pepper in a bowl and beat at medium speed for 3 minutes. Add the mayonnaise and beat for 3 more minutes. Add poppy seeds, beat until blended, Makes about 2 cups.
