Sesame Vegetables on the Grill

Ingredients

  • 2 lgeonions
  • 1 medell pepper
  • 4 cupsfresh broccoli florets
  • 1 pkg8 oz sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cupsoy sauce
  • 2 tbspeanut or vegetable oil
  • 1 tbssesame seeds

Directions

  1. Turn on grill to high.
  2. Peel the onions and cut them in half. Slice each half into crescent shaped slices 1/4 inch thick. Place the slices into a large size foil grilling bag. Seed and rinse the bell pepper and cut it in half. Cut each half into 1/4 inch strips, and add them to the bag. Add the broccoli and mushrooms to the bag.
  3. Pour the soy sauce and oil into a 2 cup glass measure and whisk to combine. Pour the mixture over the vegetables. Sprinkle in he sesame seeds. Fold the top of the bag to close it securely, and shake the bag to mix well.
  4. Place the bag flat on the grill, making sure vegetables are evenly distributed, and cook until the broccoli is crisp tender, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove to a supporting pan, and use oven mitts to cut open foil bag wit a sharp knife. Carefully fold back top of foil bag allowing steam to escape. Serve at once.
