Green Rice Casserole

Photo: Tobyotter (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cupsMonterey Jack of Swiss cheese or both shredded
  • 1 cupricotta cheese
  • 1 cupmayonnaise
  • 1/2 tspgarlic salt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 3 cupscooked rice
  • 1 pkgfrozen broccoli, thawed and drained
  • 1 cupfrozen peas, thawed
  • 1/4 cupsliced green onions

Directions

  1. Combine 1 cup Monterey Jack and next 4 ingredients in a large bowl, stir in rice, veggies and onions. Spoon into lightly greased large casserole dish. Bake: uncovered at 375 degrees for about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake 5 minutes more.
