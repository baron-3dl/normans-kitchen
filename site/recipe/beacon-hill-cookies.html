Beacon Hill Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 largeegg whites
  • 1 tspwhite vinegar
  • pinchsalt
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1 cupsemi-sweet chocolate chips, melted, cooled
  • 1/2 tspvanilla extract
  • 3/4 cupchopped pecans or walnuts

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease baking sheets. Beat egg whites, vinegar and sat on a large bowl with an electric mixer on high speed until foamy. Add the sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, beating well after each addition. Continue to beat until they are thick and glossy. Stop the mixer and add the chocolate, vanilla and nuts. Fold in with a rubber spatula. Drop batter by spoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets. Bake until set, about 10 minutes. Carefully remove from baking sheets and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely, then store in airtight containers.
