Peanut Butter Kids Cookies

Photo: pure.sugar (CC BY 2.0)

Peanut butter cookies rolled in sugar and topped with a chocolate kiss pressed into the center right after baking.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cuppacked brown sugar
  • 1/2 cupshortening
  • 1/2 cuppeanut butter
  • 1egg
  • 2 tbsmilk
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 48kisses

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except kisses. Mix at low speed until dough forms. Shape into balls (round tsp). roll into sugar. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees 10-12 minutes.
  2. Take out of oven and immediately put kiss in center.

Goes well with

  • Peanut Butter Choc. Chip Cookies
  • Peanut Butter and Honey Oatmeal Cookies
  • Chocolate Pecan Thumbprints
