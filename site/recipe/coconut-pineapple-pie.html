Coconut Pineapple Pie

Makes 6-8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cupsugar
  • 3 tbspall purpose flour
  • 1 cuplight corn syrup
  • 1 cupflaked coconut
  • 1 cancrushed pineapple, undrained
  • 3eggs, beaten
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 1unbaked pastry shell
  • 1/4 cupbutter or margarine, melted

Directions

  1. In a bowl,combine sugar and flour. Add the corn syrup, coconut, pineapple, eggs and vanilla; mix well. Pour into pastry shell. Drizzle with butter. Bake a 350 degrees for 50-55 minutes or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. (Cover loosely with foil ot the top browns to quickly). Cool on a wire rack. Chill before cutting. Store in refriogerator.
