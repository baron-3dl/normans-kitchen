Cold Spaghetti Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lbspaghetti (thin)
  • 1/2 jarSalad Supreme (McCormick's)
  • 1 smallbottle Italian dressing
  • 2tomatoes, diced tomatoes
  • 1red onion diced
  • 1green pepper, diced

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti. Cool. Mix all ingredients together. Add to spaghetti chill until ready to serve.
