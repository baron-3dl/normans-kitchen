Low Fat Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgflour tortillas
  • 1 tbsmargarine
  • 1/2 cupchopped onion
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 1 can4 oz green chilis, drained
  • 1/2 cupnon fat sour cream
  • 1 canfat free cream of chicken soup
  • 1 1/2 cupcooked, cubed chicken breast
  • 1 cup4 oz reduced fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cupskim milk

Directions

  1. Remove tortillas from refrigerator. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Melt margarine in skillet. Cook onion and garlic until tender. Stir in chili's, sour cream and soup, mix well. Reserve 3/4 sauce, set aside. Stir in chicken and 1/2 cheese to sauce in skillet. Warm tortillas according to package directions. Fill tortillas with chicken mix. Roll up, place seam side down in greased 13 x 9 baking dish. In a small bowl combine reserved sauce and milk, spoon over tortillas and top with remaining cheese. Bake 30-35 minutes until bubbly. Make 8-10 enchiladas.
