Dijon Chicken

Photo: comicpie (CC BY 2.0)

Boneless skinless chicken pieces coated in a Dijon-and-evaporated-milk dip and a Parmesan-bread crumb mixture, then baked.

Ingredients

  • 6 pcsskinless boneless chicken
  • 1/4 cupDijon mustard
  • 1/4 cupevaporated milk
  • 1/4 cupgrated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cupbread crumbs

Directions

  1. Mix mustard with milk in a bowl.
  2. Mix grated cheese with crumbs.
  3. Dip chicken pieces in mustard mix then crumb mix. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
  1. Mix the Dijon mustard with the evaporated milk in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix the grated Parmesan cheese with the bread crumbs.
  3. Dip the chicken pieces first in the mustard mixture, then in the crumb mixture.
  4. Spray a cookie sheet with cooking spray and place the chicken on it.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Tarragon Dijon Chicken
  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Honey Mustard Chicken
