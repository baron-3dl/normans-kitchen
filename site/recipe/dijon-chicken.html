Dijon Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 pcsskinless boneless chicken
  • 1/4 cupDijon mustard
  • 1/4 cupevaporated milk
  • 1/4 cupgrated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cupbread crumbs

Directions

  1. Mix mustard with milk in a bowl.
  2. Mix grated cheese with crumbs.
  3. Dip chicken pieces in mustard mix then crumb mix. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
