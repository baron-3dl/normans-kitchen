Skillet Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 lbground beef
  • 1/2 cupchopped onions
  • 1egg
  • 1slice bread crumbs
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 1 tspdry mustard
  • fat or drippings
  • 2 1/4 cupstomato juice
  • 12 cupswater
  • 1 clovearlic minced
  • 1bay leave
  • 1 tspchili powder
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 12 smonions
  • 1 bunchcarrots

Directions

  1. Mix beef with onions, egg, bread, salt, pepper and dry mustard with fork in bowl, form lightly into small balls. Brown meat balls on all sides in hot fat in large heavy skillet, remove mea balls, pour out excess grease from pan. Heat juice, water, garlic, bay leaf, chili powder, salt and pepper in same pan. Add carrots and onion, bringing to boil, reduce heat,simmer covered, 30 minutes. Add meat balls to stew, baste with sauce bring to boil.
