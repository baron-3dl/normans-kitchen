Fat Free Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgpudding
  • 1 1/2 cupscold skim milk
  • 1 envDream Whip topping mix

Directions

  1. Beat milk and topping and pudding on low speed until blended and then on high fo 4-6 minutes until soft peaks form. Enough to frost a 13 x 9 cake, refrigerate.
