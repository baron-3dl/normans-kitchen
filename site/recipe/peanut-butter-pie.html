Peanut Butter Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 8 oz.cream cheese
  • 1 cupcrunchy peanut butter
  • 1 16 oz.frozen whipped cream
  • 1 1/2 cupssifted powder sugar
  • 2 9 inchgraham cracker crusts

Directions

  1. Combine cream cheese and peanut butter, beat at medium speed till light and fluffy. Gradually add whipped topping and sugar, beat till smooth. Spoon filling into shells. Freeze 8 hours. Garnish with chocolate
