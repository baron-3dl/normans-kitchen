Molasses Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 21 oz. cansBaked Beans
  • 1/2 cupgreen pepper
  • 1 cupchopped onions
  • 1/2 cupcatsup
  • 1/3 cupmolasses
  • 2 tbspbrown sugar
  • 2 tbspmustard (jar)
  • 2 tspchili powder
  • 1/8 tspgarlic powder
  • bacon, cooked/crumbled

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients except bacon. Grease 2 quart casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes uncovered. Sprinkle bacon on and cook 5 minutes more.
