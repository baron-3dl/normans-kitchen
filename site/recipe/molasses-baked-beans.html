Molasses Baked Beans

Photo: marcelo träsel (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Canned baked beans dressed up with green pepper, onion, catsup, molasses, and spices, baked and topped with crumbled bacon.

Ingredients

  • 2 21 oz. cansBaked Beans
  • 1/2 cupgreen pepper
  • 1 cupchopped onions
  • 1/2 cupcatsup
  • 1/3 cupmolasses
  • 2 tbspbrown sugar
  • 2 tbspmustard (jar)
  • 2 tspchili powder
  • 1/8 tspgarlic powder
  • bacon, cooked/crumbled

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients except bacon. Grease 2 quart casserole. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes uncovered. Sprinkle bacon on and cook 5 minutes more.
  1. Mix all the ingredients except the bacon together.
  2. Grease a 2 quart casserole and add the bean mixture.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes, uncovered.
  4. Sprinkle the cooked, crumbled bacon on top and cook 5 minutes more.

Goes well with

  • Bar-B-Q Beans
  • Best Baked Beans
