Bev's Recipe

A one-pan baked mix of diced potatoes, onions, celery, and green peppers with hot dogs and linguica, seasoned with Tabasco and oregano.

Ingredients

  • Raw potatoes
  • onions
  • celery
  • green peppers
  • Tabasco sauce
  • oregano
  • hot dogs and linguica

Directions

  1. Spread oil on bottom of pan, dice potatoes and onions, peppers and spices and meat. Stir all together. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes.
  1. Spread oil on the bottom of a baking pan.
  2. Dice the raw potatoes, onions, and green peppers, and cut up the hot dogs and linguica. Add the celery, spices (Tabasco sauce and oregano), and the meat.
  3. Stir everything together in the pan.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Chourico Stewed w/Cabbage
  • Cacoila
  • Hot Dogs/ Sweet Sour Cabbage
