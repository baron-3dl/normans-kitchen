Bev's Recipe

Ingredients

  • Raw potatoes
  • onions
  • celery
  • green peppers
  • Tabasco sauce
  • oregano
  • hot dogs and linguica

Directions

  1. Spread oil on bottom of pan, dice potatoes and onions, peppers and spices and meat. Stir all together. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes.
