Bar-B-Q Pork Roast

Ingredients

  • 5-6 lbspork butter
  • salt
  • 1 tspchili sauce
  • 1 tspcelery seed
  • 1 cupcatsup
  • 1/4 cupWorcestershire sauce
  • 1 tspbrown sugar
  • 1/4 cupvinegar
  • 1 cupwater

Directions

  1. Cook pork in salted water over low heat for 2-2 1/2 hours. Drain and cool Save cup of broth. Cut pork in thin slices. Put in casserole dish. Combine all remaining ingredients, mix well, pour over pork, bake at 325 degrees for 30-45 minutes.
  2. FOR PULLED PORK. Shred pork with 2 forks, make sauce sauce in pan with remaining ingredients, add shredded pork, let simmer till ready to eat or make sandwiches.
