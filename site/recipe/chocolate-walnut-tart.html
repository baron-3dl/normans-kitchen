Chocolate Walnut Tart

Photo: lilivanili (CC BY 2.0)

A pecan-pie-style tart made with semisweet chocolate morsels and walnuts in a corn syrup, brown sugar, and egg filling, baked in an 11-inch tart pan.

Ingredients

  • 1refrigerator pie crust
  • 1 cupsemi sweet chocolate morsels
  • 1 cupcoarsely chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cupsalted butter
  • 1/2 cuppacked light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cupdark corn syrup
  • 3eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Fit piecrust in 11-inch tart pan. Sprinkle chocolate and walnuts evenly over crust.
  2. Melt butter. Beat in brown sugar, corn syrup, eggs and vanilla until smooth. Pour into pan.
  3. Bake in lower third of 350 degree oven 30 minutes. Cool on rack. Serve with whipped if desired.
