Potatoes Poupon

Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsDijon mustard
  • 2 tspolive oil
  • 1 clovegarlic, chopped
  • 1/2 tspItalian seasoning
  • 6 medred skin potatoes (2 lbs) cut in chunks

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients except potatoes in small bowl. Place potatoes in lightly greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan or on shallow baking sheets; toss with mustard mixture. Bake at 425 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes or until potatoes are fork tender, stirring occasionally, Makes 4 servings.
