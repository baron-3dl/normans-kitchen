Hot Crab Dip

Photo: Thai Jasmine (Smile..smile...Smile..) (CC BY 2.0)

A warm baked dip of crabmeat and cream cheese seasoned with minced onion, horseradish, salt, and pepper, served on crackers or party rye.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg6 oz frozen crab meat
  • 1 pkg8 oz. Cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsminced onions
  • 1 tsphorseradish
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Defrost and drain crabmeat well. Mix all ingredients together. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes or heat in Microwave. Serve on crackers or Pepperidge Farm party rye
  1. Defrost and drain the crabmeat well.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together.
  3. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes, or heat in the microwave.
  4. Serve on crackers or Pepperidge Farm party rye.

Goes well with

  • Stuffed Lobster Supreme
  • Mock Lobster Casserole
  • Orange Sour Cream Dip for Fruit
