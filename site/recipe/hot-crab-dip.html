Hot Crab Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg6 oz frozen crab meat
  • 1 pkg8 oz. Cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsminced onions
  • 1 tsphorseradish
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Defrost and drain crabmeat well. Mix all ingredients together. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes or heat in Microwave. Serve on crackers or Pepperidge Farm party rye
