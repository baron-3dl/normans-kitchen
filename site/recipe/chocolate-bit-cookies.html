Chocolate Bit Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cupshortening
  • 1 1/4 cupsbrown sugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 1/4 cupsflour
  • 1 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • dashsalt
  • chocolate bits

Directions

  1. Beat all ingredients until smooth. Add chocolate bits. Bake at 350 for 10-15 minutes.
