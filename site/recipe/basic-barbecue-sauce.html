Basic Barbecue Sauce

Servings: 8

Photo: dollen (CC BY 2.0)

A tangy no-cook barbecue sauce built on tomato sauce, cider vinegar, and Worcestershire, seasoned with dry mustard, chili powder, brown sugar, and dried onion.

Ingredients

  • 1Can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 3 TbsWorcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cupvegetable oil
  • 1/4 cupcider vinegar
  • 3 tspdry mustard
  • 3 Tbsbrown sugar
  • 2 tspchili powder
  • 2 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 2 Tbsdried minced onion
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tspblack pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients and let stand for at least 10 minutes.
  1. Combine all ingredients: the tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, vegetable oil, cider vinegar, dry mustard, brown sugar, chili powder, sugar, garlic powder, dried minced onion, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Let stand for at least 10 minutes.

Notes

Source: The National Turkey Federation

Goes well with

  • Barbeque Sauce
  • Molasses BBQ Sauce
  • Pulled Pork
  • Pork Barbecue on Buns
