Baked Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 ozrotini pasta
  • 1 lbground beef
  • 1 can10 1/4 oz condensed tomato soup
  • 1 clovegarlic crushed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 ozshredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Coat a 2-quart baking dish with cooking spray and set aside.
  2. Cook pasta according to directions without salt or oil.
  3. In a large nonstick skillet, cool beef over medium heat 5-6 minutes or until no longer pink. Stir and beak up the lumps of meat as you stir; drain well. Add cooked pasta, soup and optional garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon mixture into baking dish and top with cheese.
  4. Bake 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and mixture is bubbling.
