Sour Cream Cheesecake

Photo: scubadive67 (CC BY 2.0)

A baked cheesecake in a graham cracker crust, finished with a sweetened sour cream topping that is baked briefly to set.

Ingredients

  • graham cracker crust
  • 2 8 ozpkg cream cheese
  • 3eggs
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1 pintsour cream
  • 1/4 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Beat cream cheese in bowl until soft, add eggs and sugar. Beat until smooth. Pour into pie shell and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
  2. Combine sour cream, sugar and vanilla in a small bowl. Spread over baked cheesecake. Bake again for 5 minutes. Cool then refrigerate.
  1. Beat the cream cheese in a bowl until soft, then add the eggs and 1/2 cup sugar. Beat until smooth.
  2. Pour into the graham cracker pie shell and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
  3. Combine the sour cream, 1/4 cup sugar, and vanilla in a small bowl. Spread over the baked cheesecake.
  4. Bake again for 5 minutes. Cool, then refrigerate.

Goes well with

  • Cheesecake
  • E-Z 3 Step Cheese Cake
  • Individual Cheese Cakes
  • Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake
