Sour Cream Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • graham cracker crust
  • 2 8 ozpkg cream cheese
  • 3eggs
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1 pintsour cream
  • 1/4 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Beat cream cheese in bowl until soft, add eggs and sugar. Beat until smooth. Pour into pie shell and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
  2. Combine sour cream, sugar and vanilla in a small bowl. Spread over baked cheesecake. Bake again for 5 minutes. Cool then refrigerate.
