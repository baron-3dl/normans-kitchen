Chicken Cacciatore

Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4chicken breast halves skinned w/or/wo bones
  • 2 tbspolive oil
  • 1green bell pepper cut into rings
  • 1 smallonion sliced
  • 1 clovegarlic minced
  • 1 can 14 oz.Italian style stewed tomatoes
  • 1 can 6 oz.Italian style tomato paste
  • 3/4 cupdry red wine or chicken broth
  • 1bay leaf
  • 1 tspdried Italian seasoning
  • Spaghetti

Directions

  1. BROWN chicken in hot oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat 5 minutes on each side. Remove chicken, reserve drippings in pan. Add bell pepper and onion to hot drippings and saute until tender.
  2. RETURN chicken to pan; add garlic and next 5 ingedients. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 45 minutes. Discard bay leaf. Serveover spaghetti.

Directions

When chicken is done add wine, vegetables, mushroom, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco, little salt and pepper and tomato sauce. Cover and cook about 15 minutes on low heat. Serve over spaghetti
