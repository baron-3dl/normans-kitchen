Barbecued Pork Loin

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 1 5 lbpork loin
  • 1/4 cupsugar
  • 2 tsppaprika
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 1 tsponion powder
  • 1 bsWorcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tspTabasco
  • 1/2 cuplemon juice
  • 1 cupvinegar
  • 1 cupwater

Directions

  1. Place pork loin in roasting pan. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, paprika, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Worcestershire sauce, pepper sauce, lemon juice, vinegar and water, bring to boiling point. Spoon some sauce over meat. Roast pork loin at 325 degrees for about 2 to 2 1/2 hours or to an internal temperature of at least 160 on a meat thermometer. Baste pork frequently with the sauce. Pork loin roast serves 6
