Bar-B-Q Beans

Photo: The DLC (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A baked bean casserole combining browned ground beef with canned beans and kidney beans in a tangy barbecue sauce seasoned with onion soup mix, catsup, mustard, and instant coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb.Hamburger
  • 1 env.Onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 1 cupCatsup
  • 2 tbspMustard (from jar)
  • 1 tspcider vinegar
  • 2 16 oz. canBeans
  • 1 16 oz. canKidney Beans
  • 2 tspInstant Coffee
  • 2-3 tbspBrown Sugar

Directions

  1. Cook hamburg til brown or well done, drain grease, add all ingredients to pot, blend well for a few minutes, pour into casserole dish and bake at 400 degrees for 30 min.
  1. Cook the hamburger until brown or well done, then drain off the grease.
  2. Add all the remaining ingredients to the pot and blend well for a few minutes.
  3. Pour into a casserole dish and bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Molasses Baked Beans
  • Best Baked Beans
