Light Gelatin Dessert

Photo: suavehouse113 (CC BY 2.0)

A chilled molded dessert of lime gelatin with crushed pineapple, small-curd cottage cheese, and whipped heavy cream folded in.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkglime jello
  • 1/3 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupboiling water
  • 1 cancrushed pineapples
  • cottage cheese
  • heavy cream

Directions

  1. Dissolve jello and sugar in boiling water. Add 1 cup well drained pineapples. Let stand until syrupy. Add 1 cup smallcurd cottage cheese and 1 cup heavy cream, whipped very stiff and fold in carefully. Let set.
  1. Dissolve the jello and sugar in the boiling water.
  2. Add 1 cup well-drained crushed pineapple. Let stand until syrupy.
  3. Add 1 cup small-curd cottage cheese and 1 cup heavy cream, whipped very stiff, and fold in carefully.
  4. Let set.
