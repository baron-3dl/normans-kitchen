Zucchini or Summer Squash Casserole

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Boil together: 4 medium squash and 1/4 cup chopped onion then Drain.
  2. Stir together and add to squash mixture the following: 1 can cream of chicken or mushroom soup and 1 pint of sour cream.
  3. Mix together: 1 stick butter or margarine (melted) to 1 8 oz pkg Pepperidge Farm Herb Stuffing mix.
  4. Spread half of stuffing mixture in bottom of casserole dish. Cover with squash, sour cream mixture and then top with remaining stuffing mix. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.
