Cole Slaw

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cupvinegar
  • 1/2 cupoil
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 medcabbages
  • 1 tspcelery seed
  • green or red pepper, chopped

Directions

  1. Mix first 4 ingredients. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes. Cool
  2. Mix all together and refrigerate overnite. Will keep for 10 days.
