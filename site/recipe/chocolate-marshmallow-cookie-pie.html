Chocolate Marshmallow Cookie Pie

Photo: kurmanstaff (CC BY 2.0)

A no-bake chocolate pudding pie in a prepared chocolate crust, layered with vanilla wafers and a marshmallow-whipped topping mixture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cupsminiature marshmallows
  • 2 tbsmilk
  • 2 1/2 cupscool whip cream
  • 2 cupscold milk
  • 2 pkg(4 serve) Chocolate flavor instant pudding
  • 1Chocolate Pie Crust (Prepared)
  • 14vanilla wafers

Directions

  1. Microwave marshmallows and 2 tbs milk in medium microwave bowl on High 45 seconds. Stir. Marshmallows will be partially melted.) Refrigerate for 15 minutes to cool. Stir in 1 cup whipped topping.
  2. Pour 2 cups milk into large bowl. Add pudding mixes. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes. Let stand 1 minute or until thickened. Sir in remaining 1 1/2 cup whipped topping. Spoon into crust. Arrange cookies over pudding mixture. Spread marshmallow mixture over cookies.
  3. Refrigerate 4 hours. Drizzle with chocolate topping before serving if you have any.
  1. Microwave the marshmallows and 2 tablespoons milk in a medium microwave bowl on High for 45 seconds, then stir. (The marshmallows will be partially melted.) Refrigerate for 15 minutes to cool, then stir in 1 cup of the whipped topping.
  2. Pour 2 cups cold milk into a large bowl. Add the pudding mixes and beat with a wire whisk for 2 minutes. Let stand 1 minute, or until thickened. Stir in the remaining 1 1/2 cups whipped topping.
  3. Spoon the pudding mixture into the prepared chocolate crust. Arrange the vanilla wafers over the pudding mixture, then spread the marshmallow mixture over the cookies.
  4. Refrigerate 4 hours. Drizzle with chocolate topping before serving, if you have any.

Goes well with

  • Chocolate Cheesecake Pie
  • Chocolate Silk Pie
