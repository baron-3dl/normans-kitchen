Molasses BBQ Sauce

Makes 4 cups

A tangy-sweet barbecue sauce simmered from cider vinegar, molasses, Worcestershire sauce, and spices, then strained. Makes 4 cups.

Ingredients

  • 1 cupcider vinegar
  • 1 tbsgrated ginger
  • 2 tbsdry mustard
  • 1 1/4 cupsketchup
  • 5 tbsWorcestershire sauce
  • 1 clovegarlic, crushed ice
  • 1 cupmolasses
  • 1lemon, sliced
  • 3 tbsbutter, melted
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cuplemon juice

Directions

  1. Using an enamel or stainless steel sauce pan, combine the vinegar, ginger, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, molasses and lemon slices. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to a simmer. Cook 20 minutes. Stir in butter and cook 5 minutes more. Remove from heat and add salt and lemon juice. Allow to cool and strain before sing, Makes 4 cups.
  2. Like so many sauces, this one gets better with age. It should keep, refrigerated, for several weeks.
  3. The secret to good barbecuing is to add the sauce during the last few minutes of cooking, otherwise the high sugar content burns before the meat or poultry is done.
