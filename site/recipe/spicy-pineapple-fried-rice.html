Spicy Pineapple Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsvegetable oil
  • 1 clovegarlic, crushed
  • 1 tspfinely chopped fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tspcrushed red pepper
  • 3 cupscooke rice
  • 1/4 cupsoy sauce
  • 1 can8 1/4 oz crushed pineapples in syrup, drained
  • 1/4 cupthinly sliced onions

Directions

  1. In large nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium low heat until hot. Add garlic, ginger and red pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
  2. Increase heat to medium high. Add rice and soy sauce; cook 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Add pineapple, tossing to combine; heat through.
  3. Sprinkle with green onions before serving.
