Spinach Balls

Ingredients

  • 2 pkg10 oz. frozen chopped spinach, thawed, squeezed dry
  • 2 cupsherb stuffing mix (crushed)
  • 1 cupfreshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cupmelted butter
  • 3eggs
  • 4small green onions finely chopped
  • dashof nutmeg

Directions

  1. Thoroughly mix all ingredients together and shape into 1 inch balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes
