Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupfine breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cupgrated parmesan cheese
  • 4-6chicken breasts
  • 1egg, beaten
  • 3 tbspmargarine
  • 1green pepper sliced thin
  • 1 8 oz. cantomato sauce
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 1/4 tsporegano
  • 1 cupmozzarella shredded

Directions

  1. Combine crumbs and parmesan. Dip chicken in egg and coat with crumbs. Cook chicken in skillet 3 minutes on each side til brown. Arrange pepper oven chicken. Combine remaining ingredients pour over chicken reduce heat to 220 degrees cook for 20-25 min. till tender. sprinkle with mozzarella til cheese melts.
