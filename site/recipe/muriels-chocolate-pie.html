Muriel's Chocolate Pie

Ingredients

  • 1unbaked pie shell
  • 12 cansweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 2eggs, beaten
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • pinchsalt
  • 2 pkgGerman baking chocolate
  • 1/2 cupshredded coconut or slivered almonds

Directions

  1. Mix everything together in double boiler until chocolate is melted, pour into shell, sprinkle coconut on top and bake at 400 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Serve warm or cold. Refrigerate leftovers.
