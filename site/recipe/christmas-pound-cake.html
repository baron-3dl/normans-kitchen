Christmas Pound Cake

Photo: kinwart (CC BY 2.0)

A lemon-flavored pound cake studded with candied pineapple, cherries, and pecans, finished with a warm lemon glaze that is drizzled into holes poked in the cake and soaked in.

Ingredients

  • 2 cupssugar
  • 1 cupmargarine
  • 6eggs
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 4 tsplemon extract
  • candied pineapples and cherries
  • chopped pecans
  • 2 tbsflour
  • Glaze
  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 1/2 cuplemon juice
  • 1/2 tbslemon rind

Directions

  1. Cream together sugar and margarine, add eggs. Add flour and flavorings and mix thoroughly, add fruits and cuts (that have been dredged in flour). Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.
  2. For glaze heat all ingredients until sugar melts, make holes in cake and drizzle on top and let soak in.
  1. Cream together the sugar and margarine, then add the eggs.
  2. Add the flour and flavorings (vanilla and lemon extract) and mix thoroughly.
  3. Add the candied pineapples, cherries, and chopped pecans that have been dredged in the 2 tablespoons flour.
  4. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.
  5. Make the glaze: heat all the glaze ingredients (margarine, sugar, lemon juice, and lemon rind) until the sugar melts.
  6. Make holes in the cake, drizzle the glaze on top, and let it soak in.

Goes well with

  • German Nut Cake
