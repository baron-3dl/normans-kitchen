Chocolate Kahlua Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cuplight corn syrup
  • 1/2 cupfirmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cupbutter
  • 2 1 ozsquares unsweetened chocolate chips
  • 3eggs
  • 2 tbsKahlua or other coffee liqueur
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cupspecans pieces
  • 1 9-inchunbaked pie shell

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, bring corn syrup, brown sugar, and butter to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. Stir in chocolate until melted and smooth; cool slightly. In medium bowl,whisk eggs with Kahlua and vanilla. Slowly whisk in chocolate mixture. Stir in pecans. Pour into crust. Bake on bottom rack in 350 degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. If crust begins to get to dark, cover outer edges with foil. Serve warm or at room temperature with Kahlua whipped cream. Store in refrigerator.
  2. For Kahlua whipped cream in a small bowl, beat 1 cup whipping cream, 3 tablespoons powdered sugar and 1 tbs of Kahlua until soft peaks form. Store in airtight container in fridge.
