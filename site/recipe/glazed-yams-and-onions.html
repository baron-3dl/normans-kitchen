Glazed Yams and Onions

Cooked yams and small white onions simmered together in a brown sugar and butter glaze until richly golden.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupbutter
  • 1 cupbrown sugar
  • 1/4 cupwater
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 8 medsized yams, cooked, peeled and halved
  • 1 lbsmall white onions peeled and cooked

Directions

  1. Melt butter in large frying pan, stir in brown sugar, water and salt, heat slowly until sugar is melted.
  2. Arrange potatoes and onions in pans, simmer, basting often until glazed to a rich golden color.
