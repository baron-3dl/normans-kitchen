Chocolate Peanut Butter Drop Cookies

Photo: Crystl (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 1/2 cupmargarine
  • 1/2 cupchunky peanut butter
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • semi sweet tid bits

Directions

  1. In a large bowl at low speed, beat 1 tbs water and all ingredients except tid bits until mixed, increase speed to medium and beat 2 minutes,scrape bowl add tid bits. Drop by spoonfuls about 2 inch apart. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
