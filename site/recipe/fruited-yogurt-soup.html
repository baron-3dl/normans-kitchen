Fruited Yogurt Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 cupsplain yogurt
  • 2 medpeaches, peeled etc
  • 1 cupsliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cuporange juice
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 4 tspsugar
  • 2 tsphoney
  • mint leaves

Directions

  1. Blend ingredients. Chilled at least 2 hours.
