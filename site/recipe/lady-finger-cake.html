Lady Finger Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pkg4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 2 pintsheavy cream
  • 1 tspconfectioners sugar
  • 3 pkgLady Fingers
  • fruit topping

Directions

  1. Combine cream cheese and sugar.
  2. In another bowl combine cream and confectioners sugar. Mix both together. Put Lady Fingers on bottom and sides of pan, pour mix on top and choice of topping. Refrigerate.
