Chicken Salad

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cupscubed cooked chicken
  • 1 cupmayonnaise
  • 1/4 cupsour cream
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • dashpepper
  • 2 cupscooked rice
  • 1 cupchopped celery
  • 1/2 cupchopped green peppers
  • 1/4 cupchopped onions
  • 1 can20 oz. pineapple tidbit, drained
  • 1 can8 oz sliced water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 cup4 oz shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cupsliced almonds, toasted
  • Cantaloupe, optional

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients. Fold in rice, celery, green pepper, onion, pineapple, water chestnuts and cheese. Add almonds just before serving if desired. Serve on cantaloupe halves or wedges if desired, or use as a sandwich spread.
