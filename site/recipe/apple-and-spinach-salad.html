Apple and Spinach Salad

Makes 6 servings

Photo: Dan4th (CC BY 2.0)

A baby spinach salad with thinly sliced apple, red onion and crumbled blue cheese, tossed in a citrus-Dijon-honey dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsfresh orange juice
  • 2 tbslime juice
  • 2 tspDijon mustard
  • 2 tsphoney
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tspblack pepper
  • 1/2 cupthinly sliced red onions
  • 8 cupsbagged prewashed baby spinach (8 oz)
  • 1 lgesweet tart apple, cored and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cupcrumbled blue cheese

Directions

  1. Combine first 6 ingredients, stirring well with whisk.
  2. Combine onion, spinach, and apple in a large bowl. Drizzle with dressing, toss gently to coat. Sprinkle with cheese.
