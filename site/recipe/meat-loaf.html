Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs.hamburger
  • 1 cupquick cooking oats (oatmeal)
  • 1 cupmilk
  • 2 medcarrots
  • 1 medonions
  • 5 sprigsfresh parley (or flakes)
  • 6celery leaves (or celery salt)
  • 1/4green pepper (optional)
  • 2 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1 tsppoultry seasoning
  • 2eggs
  • 1/3 cupketchup

Directions

  1. Mix meat and oatmeal thoroughly. Put all other ingredients in a blender, blend until thoroughly mixed. Add to meat mixture, put in a loaf pan and make loaf. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 1/2 hours or until done.
