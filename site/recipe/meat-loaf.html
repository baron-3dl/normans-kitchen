Meat Loaf

Photo: jbcurio (CC BY 2.0)

A baked beef meat loaf that uses quick oats and milk as a binder, with carrots, onion, celery, and green pepper blended in and seasoned with poultry seasoning and ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs.hamburger
  • 1 cupquick cooking oats (oatmeal)
  • 1 cupmilk
  • 2 medcarrots
  • 1 medonions
  • 5 sprigsfresh parley (or flakes)
  • 6celery leaves (or celery salt)
  • 1/4green pepper (optional)
  • 2 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1 tsppoultry seasoning
  • 2eggs
  • 1/3 cupketchup

Directions

  1. Mix meat and oatmeal thoroughly. Put all other ingredients in a blender, blend until thoroughly mixed. Add to meat mixture, put in a loaf pan and make loaf. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 1/2 hours or until done.
  1. Mix the hamburger and quick cooking oats thoroughly.
  2. Put all the other ingredients in a blender and blend until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Add the blended mixture to the meat mixture.
  4. Put in a loaf pan and shape into a loaf.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 1/2 hours, or until done.

Goes well with

  • Individual Meat Loaves
  • Hamburger Layer Cake
