Potatoes & Broccoli au Gratin

Photo: Red Lobster Lover Joe twitter:RLLoverJoe (Public Domain)

A lightened potato-and-broccoli casserole: chunky mashed baking potatoes blended with ricotta, fat-free sour cream, dill, and broccoli, topped with fat-free Cheddar and baked.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbspeeled and halved baking potatoes
  • 1 cupchopped fresh broccoli
  • 1/2 cupchopped onions
  • 1/2 cupskim ricotta cheese
  • 1 tspdried dill
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tspground red peppers
  • 1 cupfat free sour cream
  • 3/4 cupshredded fat free Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. Coat 7 by 11 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Cook potatoes in boiling water 15-20 minutes. Drain over a bowl to reserve 1 cup cooking liquid. Return potatoes and liquid to pan; mash until slightly chunky. Stir in broccoli, onion, ricotta, dill, salt, red pepper, sour cream. Spoon mixture into dish. Bake 35 minutes. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Bake 5 minutes or until cheese melts.
  1. Heat the oven to 375 degrees. Coat a 7 by 11 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Cook the potatoes in boiling water 15-20 minutes. Drain over a bowl to reserve 1 cup of the cooking liquid.
  3. Return the potatoes and the reserved liquid to the pan; mash until slightly chunky.
  4. Stir in the broccoli, onion, ricotta, dill, salt, red pepper, and sour cream.
  5. Spoon the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  6. Bake 35 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle with the shredded Cheddar cheese. Bake 5 minutes more, or until the cheese melts.
