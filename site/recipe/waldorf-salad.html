Waldorf Salad

Photo: Windslash (CC BY 2.0)

A Waldorf-style salad of diced cooked chicken, celery, apples, and walnuts served on shredded romaine and drizzled with a honey-mayonnaise poppy seed dressing.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Romaine lettuce
  • 1 lbcooked chicken breasts, cooled and diced
  • 1 cupthinly sliced or chopped celery
  • 1 cup1/2 cubed apples
  • 1 cupchopped walnuts
  • Fresh strawberries to garnish
  • Waldorf Dressing
  • 1 cupmayonnaise
  • 2 ozapple cider vinegar
  • 2 ozhoney
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 18 tspcinnamon
  • 1/2 tsppoppy seeds

Directions

  1. Combine dressing ingredients in a bowl.
  2. On four salad plates, make bed of shredded romaine. Mix celery, apples, walnuts and diced chicken. Spoon onto lettuce. Drizzle dressing over top of each serving.
  3. Garnish with fresh strawberries and serve with a fresh croissant.
  1. Combine the dressing ingredients (mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey, salt, cinnamon, and poppy seeds) in a bowl.

Goes well with

  • Chicken Salad
  • Apple and Spinach Salad
