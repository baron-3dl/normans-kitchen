Hot Potato Salad with Franks

A hot potato salad casserole that layers seasoned sliced cooked potatoes with thinly sliced onions and split franks, baked until warmed through.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Combine 4 cups thinly sliced cooked potatoes with 1/3 cup salad oil, 3 tbs vinegar and 1 1/2 tsp salt and a dash of pepper. arrange alternate layers of salad mixture, thinly sliced onions and franks cut lengthwise in a 1 1/2 quart casserole. Cover casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
  1. Combine 4 cups thinly sliced cooked potatoes with 1/3 cup salad oil, 3 tablespoons vinegar, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, and a dash of pepper.
  2. In a 1 1/2-quart casserole, arrange alternating layers of the potato salad mixture, thinly sliced onions, and franks cut lengthwise.
  3. Cover the casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Warm Potato Salad
  • Hot Dogs/ Sweet Sour Cabbage
  • 1-2-3 Potato Salad
