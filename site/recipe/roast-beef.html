Roast Beef

Ingredients

  • 1Sirloin Tip Roast
  • 2 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1 medonion, chopped
  • 2 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cupsbrewed coffee
  • 2 1/2 cupswater divided
  • 2beef cubes
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 2 tspdried basil
  • 1/2 tspsalt and pepper
  • 1/2 cupflour

Directions

  1. In Dutch oven brown beef on all sides in oil, remove set aside add garlic and onion, saute 5 minutes. Add coffee, 2 cups water, bouillon, salt and pepper, basil. Return meat, bring to boil, then simmer covered 2 1/2 hours or until done. Remove meat, mix flour and 1/2 cup water, whisk into drippings until thickened, slice cut roast, pour gravy over and serve.
