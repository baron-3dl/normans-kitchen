Cream Cheese Party Cupcakes

Photo: tawest64 (CC BY 2.0)

Mini cheesecake-style cupcakes: a cream cheese, sugar, and egg batter baked in paper liners, then topped with a sweetened sour cream mixture and strawberry preserves.

Ingredients

  • 3 8 oz.cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 5eggs
  • 1 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 1 8 oz.sour cream
  • 1/4 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla
  • Strawberry preserves

Directions

  1. Beat cream cheese in large bowl until soft and creamy, gradually add sugar until light and fluffy, add eggs one at a time. Beat well after each egg. Add 1 1/2 tsp vanilla. Spoon mixture into paper cupcakes 3/4 full. Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine sour cream with sugar and vanilla stirring well. Spoon 1/4 tsp sour cream mixture on each cupcake, add strawberry jam, cook 5 minutes more.
  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until soft and creamy. Gradually add 1 cup sugar and beat until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Add 1 1/2 tsp vanilla.
  3. Spoon the mixture into paper cupcake liners, filling each 3/4 full.
  4. Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
  5. Combine the sour cream with 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp vanilla, stirring well.
  6. Spoon 1/4 tsp of the sour cream mixture on each cupcake and add strawberry preserves. Bake 5 minutes more.

Goes well with

  • Individual Cheese Cakes
  • Cheesecake
  • E-Z 3 Step Cheese Cake
  • Sour Cream Cheesecake
