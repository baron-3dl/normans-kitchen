Fluffy Chocolate Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupbutter
  • 4 cupssifted powder sugar
  • 3/4 cupcocoa
  • 1/2 cupevaporated milk
  • 1 tspGrand Marnier or other orange flavor liqueur

Directions

  1. All all ingredients in order in processor. Put cover on. Pulse 2-3 times. Process for 1 minute scraping sides.
