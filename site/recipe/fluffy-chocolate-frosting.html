Fluffy Chocolate Frosting

Photo: ginnerobot (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A food-processor chocolate frosting of butter, powdered sugar, cocoa, evaporated milk, and orange liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupbutter
  • 4 cupssifted powder sugar
  • 3/4 cupcocoa
  • 1/2 cupevaporated milk
  • 1 tspGrand Marnier or other orange flavor liqueur

Directions

  1. All all ingredients in order in processor. Put cover on. Pulse 2-3 times. Process for 1 minute scraping sides.
  1. Add all ingredients in order into a processor and put the cover on.
  2. Pulse 2-3 times, then process for 1 minute, scraping the sides.

Goes well with

  • Creamy Chocolate Frosting
  • Fat Free Frosting
  • Rocky Road Frosting
  • Soft Frosting
