German Streusel Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgyellow cake mix
  • 1 cupwater
  • 1/4 cupvegetable oil
  • 3eggs
  • Filling
  • 2 cupsgraham cracker crumbs
  • 3/4 cupnuts
  • 3/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tspcinnamon
  • 3/4 cupbutter, melted
  • Vanilla Glaze

Directions

  1. Prepare filling by mixing all ingredients together, should be crumbly.
  2. Prepare cake mix. Pour half of cake mix in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan, sprinkle with some of filling mix, then layer of cake mix, then layer of crumb mix and bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Drizzle vanilla glaze on top when cool.
