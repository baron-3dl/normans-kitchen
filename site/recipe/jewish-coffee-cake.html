Jewish Coffee Cake

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A sour cream coffee cake baked in a tube pan with a cinnamon-walnut filling layered inside and on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 cupshortening
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 3eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 3 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • Filling
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupwalnuts chopped
  • 1 1/2 tspcinnamon

Directions

  1. Cream shortening, add ingredients one at a time. Use greased tub pan. Spread filling between layers and on top. Bake at 375 degrees about 45 minutes.
  1. Cream the shortening, then add the remaining batter ingredients (sugar, eggs, vanilla, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sour cream) one at a time.
  2. For the filling, combine 3/4 cup sugar, 3/4 cup chopped walnuts, and 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon.
  3. Grease a tube pan. Spread the filling between the layers of batter and on top.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for about 45 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Miniature Coffee Cakes
  • Sour Cream Cranberry Cake
