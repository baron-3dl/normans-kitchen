Jewish Coffee Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cupshortening
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 3eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 3 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • Filling
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupwalnuts chopped
  • 1 1/2 tspcinnamon

Directions

  1. Cream shortening, add ingredients one at a time. Use greased tub pan. Spread filling between layers and on top. Bake at 375 degrees about 45 minutes.
