Warm Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupsbacon chopped in 1 in. squares
  • 1/2 cupolive oil
  • 3/4 tspthyme, powdered
  • 2 clovesgarlic, crushed
  • 1/2 tsporegano
  • 3/4 tspsalt
  • 1 pinchpepper
  • 1/2 tspbasil
  • 1/2 cupchopped parsley
  • 1/2 cupred onion, sliced 1 inch strips
  • 1/2red pepper sliced in 2 inch strips
  • 1 cupolive oil
  • 8 cupsred potatoes
  • 2 tspchicken stock powder (optional)

Directions

  1. Boil the potatoes until they seem almost done, drain, set aside and let cool to room temperature.
  2. Place the bacon in a saute pan and add the 1/2 cup of olive oil and saute the bacon until it is cooked (but not browned). It should be tender. Continue to simmer while adding the seasonings to the bacon, thyme, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper and basil.
  3. Simmer together for five minutes.
  4. This will allow the spices to blend and become stronger in flavor.
  5. Next remove the bacon mixture from the saute pan and place in a large bowl. Add the last cup of olive oil, stir in the parsley and red onions. Now begin quartering the room temperature red potatoes into the bacon mixture.
  6. Fold the bacon and seasoning mixture over the potatoes, blending gently so you don't crush the potatoes. Sprinkle the red peppers over the potato salad as a garnish. Let the salad rest for one hour before serving, this will allow the seasoning to work through the salad.
  7. If you would like a little extra flavor or just something a little different, add the 2 tsp of chicken stock powder when mixing the list of seasonings together.
  8. his is a great tasting potato salad, with the flavor getting even better the next day.
  9. Then to give this salad the introduction it deserves,scoop a portion of your potato salad onto romaine lettuce leaf for a nice look. Or really have some fun and slice and English cucumber in half lengthwise, hollow out the halves and the place on the cucumber. Wow.
