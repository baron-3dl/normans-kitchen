Sour Cream Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cupCrisco
  • 2 cupssugar
  • 2eggs, well beaten
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • 3 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 tspmace
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Beat crisco with sugar, add eggs and beat well, add sour cream mix well.
  2. Mix flour, soda, mace and add to egg mixture, add vanilla and mix well.
  3. Drop by spoonful on cookie sheet, press down each cookie with wet tongs of fork. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.

Sour Cream Cookies · 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cupCrisco
  • 2 cupssugar
  • 2eggs well beaten
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • 3 cupsflour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 tspmace
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Beat with mixer the crisco, sugar and eggs until well creamed then add sour cream and mix well. Add the baking soda and mace to the flour then blend into the wet mixture when blended add the vanilla and blend a little longer.
  2. Drop on cookie sheet by tablespoonful about 2 inches apart . . . press down each cookie with wet tines of fork. Bake a 350 degrees for about 10 minutes until light brown. Cool in pan slightly then to a rack.
