Spicy Marinated Eye Round

Ingredients

  • 3-5 lbsEye Round
  • 3onions sliced
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • SPICY SAUCE
  • 2 cupsketchup
  • 2 cupswater
  • 2 lgeonions, sliced
  • 1/3 cupwine vinegar
  • 1/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 2 tbspWorcestershire sauce
  • 1 tspdry mustard
  • 1 tsporegano
  • 1 tsppepper
  • 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 1/2 tspchili powder
  • 1/2 tspground cloves
  • 1/4 tspground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tspTabasco
  • 1Bay leaf

Directions

  1. Place roast in pan, add onions, salt and pepper. Bake covered at 325 degrees for 3 hours and 30 minutes. Cool and slice thinly.
  2. Place roast in casserole dish, add spicy sauce, cover and chill for 8 hours. When ready remove from fridge and let stand for 30 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
  3. SPICY SAUCE
  4. Bring all ingredients to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 1 hour. Remove Bay leaf.
