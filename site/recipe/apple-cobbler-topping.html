Apple Cobbler Topping

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupmargarine softened
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1egg
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 1 cupflour
  • 1 cupold-fashioned oats
  • 3/4 tspcinnamon

Directions

  1. Using an electric mixer on medium speed, beat margarine with sugar in a large bowl till creamy. blend in egg and vanilla. Stir in flour, oats and cinnamon thoroughly.
  2. Spoon crumbly mixture onto apple mixture. Bake at 350 for about 45 minutes
