Emeril's Essence

Photo: thetejon (CC BY-SA 2.0)

An all-purpose Creole-style seasoning blend of sweet paprika, garlic and onion powder, oregano, thyme, and peppers.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbspsweet paprika
  • 1/4 cupsalt
  • 1/4 cupgarlic powder
  • 2 tbspdried oregano
  • 2 tbspdried thyme
  • 2 tbsponion powder
  • 2 tbsfresh ground pepper
  • 2 tbspground red pepper
