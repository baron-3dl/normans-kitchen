Sukiyaki

Photo: ajari (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 4 tbscooking oil
  • 4 cupscelery
  • 2sweet onions
  • 1 cupgreen onions
  • 3 cupsmushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cupbamboo shoots, diced
  • 2tomatoes sliced thin
  • 1beef bouillon cube dissolved in 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cupsoy sauce
  • 1 qtspinach leaves bite size
  • 1 lbsteak, thinly sliced

Directions

  1. Heat oil in electric skillet set at medium heat. Add all vegetables except spinach and cook for a few minutes. Sprinkle tomatoes over top so hey stay crisp. Pour consomme or bouillon, soy sauce and sugar over vegetables and stir. Add the spinach and steak. Simmer for a while, serve hot from skillet.
