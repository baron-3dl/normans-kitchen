Baked Chicken Thighs

Photo: jencu (CC BY 2.0)

Chicken thighs dipped in mustard butter, coated in a Parmesan and breadcrumb mixture, and baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsbutter or margarine
  • 1 tbsprepared mustard seed
  • 3/4 cupbreadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cupParmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsparsley
  • salt and pepper
  • 8chicken thighs

Directions

  1. Melt butter and add mustard.
  2. Combine bread crumbs, parsley, cheese, salt and pepper.
  3. Dip chicken in butter mixture, roll in bread crumbs, place in greased 9 x 13 baking pan. Drizzle with remaining butter. Bake at 400 degrees for about 45 minutes.
  1. Melt the butter and add the mustard.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the bread crumbs, parsley, cheese, salt and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken thigh in the butter mixture, then roll in the bread crumb mixture.
  4. Place the coated thighs in a greased 9 x 13 baking pan.
  5. Drizzle with the remaining butter.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees for about 45 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Chicken Parmesan
  • Corn Flake Chicken
  • Savory Baked Chicken
