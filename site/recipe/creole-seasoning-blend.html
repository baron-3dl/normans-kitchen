Creole Seasoning Blend

Makes 1/4 cup

Ingredients

  • 1 tbspsalt
  • 1 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsponion powder
  • 1 1/2 tsppaprika
  • 1 1/4 tspdried thyme
  • 1 tspred peppers
  • 3/4 tspblack pepper
  • 3/4 tspdried oregano
  • 1/2 tspground bay leaves
  • 1/4 tspchili powder

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients; store in an airtight container. Use with seafood, chicken, beef, or vegetables.
