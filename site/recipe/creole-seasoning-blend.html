Creole Seasoning Blend

Makes 1/4 cup

Photo: krysaia (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A dry Creole spice mix of salt, garlic and onion powder, paprika, thyme, and peppers for seasoning seafood, chicken, beef, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbspsalt
  • 1 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsponion powder
  • 1 1/2 tsppaprika
  • 1 1/4 tspdried thyme
  • 1 tspred peppers
  • 3/4 tspblack pepper
  • 3/4 tspdried oregano
  • 1/2 tspground bay leaves
  • 1/4 tspchili powder

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients; store in an airtight container. Use with seafood, chicken, beef, or vegetables.
  1. Combine all ingredients: the salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, dried thyme, red pepper, black pepper, dried oregano, ground bay leaves, and chili powder.
  2. Store in an airtight container. Use with seafood, chicken, beef, or vegetables.

Goes well with

  • Emeril's Essence
  • Creole Sauce
  • Shrimp Creole
  • Creole Rice
