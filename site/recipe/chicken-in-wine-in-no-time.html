Chicken in Wine in No Time

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupflour
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 2-3 lbschicken
  • 1/4 cupoil
  • 1 pkgdry onion soup mix
  • 1 cupdry white wine
  • water

Directions

  1. Put flour, salt, pepper in paper bag, add chicken and shake to coat, place in fry pan in oil and brown well. Sprinkle with onion soup, pour in wine. Add a little water, cover and simmer until tender, about 45 minutes.
