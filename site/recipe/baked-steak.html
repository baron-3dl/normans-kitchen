Baked Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 lbround steak
  • 2 tbspflour
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 2 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1 can14 oz. stewed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cupchopped carrots
  • 1/4 cupchopped celery
  • 1 tbspchopped onions
  • 1/4 tspWorcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbspcheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Cut meat into 3 or four pieces coat with flour and salt. Brown meat in skillet with oil, on both sides, remove and place in casserole dish. Add vegetables to pan with Worcestershire sauce, bring to boil over medium heat, cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour over meat. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hour till tender. Sprinkle cheese over meat and heat till melted.
