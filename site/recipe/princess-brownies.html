Princess Brownies

Photo: kippster (CC BY 2.0)

A layered brownie made from a boxed brownie mix with a sweetened cream cheese layer baked in the middle.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgBrownie mix, family size
  • 1 pkg8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/3 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 1egg

Directions

  1. Prepare mix as directed, set aside. Combine softened cream cheese and sugar, mix well. Stir in vanilla and egg. Spread half brownie mix into greased 13 x 9 pan cover with cream cheese mixture and spoon remaining brownie batter on top. Bake at 350 degrees 35-40 minutes.
  1. Prepare the brownie mix as directed on the package; set aside.
  2. Combine the softened cream cheese and sugar; mix well. Stir in the vanilla and egg.
  3. Spread half of the brownie mix into a greased 13 x 9 pan. Cover with the cream cheese mixture, then spoon the remaining brownie batter on top.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Cheese Brownies
  • Brownies
  • Double Dark Decadent Brownie
