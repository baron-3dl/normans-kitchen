Chocolate Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 ozsweet baking chocolate broken in pieces
  • 2 tbspbutter or margarine
  • 1/3 cupsugar
  • 1 cuplight or dark corn syrup
  • 3eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 1/2 cupspecan halves
  • 9 inchunbaked deep dish pie shell
  • Cream for topping optional

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees,
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in pan for 1 or 2 minutes, stir until is completely melted and blended.
  3. Stir in sugar, corn syrup, eggs and vanilla until well blended. Stir in pecans. Pour into pie shell. Bake 50 minutes or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean. Cool on wire rack. Serve with whipped topping.
