Cream Cheese Rollups

Ingredients

  • 1 loafdiet bread (thin sliced)
  • 8 ozcream cheese
  • t tbspmilk
  • 1 tspvanilla extract or any other flavor
  • 1 1/2 sticksmelted butter or margarine
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • cinnamon

Directions

  1. Mix cream cheese with milk and extract. Cut crust off bread to make square. Spread a little cream cheese on each and roll up. Coat each roll with melted butter and roll in sugar/cinnamon mixture. Freeze before baking. Bake at 350 degrees for only a few minutes until lightly brown.
