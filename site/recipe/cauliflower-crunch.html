Cauliflower Crunch

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsolive oil or veg oil
  • 8sun-dried tomatoes, cut into strips
  • 2 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 1 can4.5 oz chopped green chili's, drained
  • 2 pkg10 oz Frozen cauliflower in cheese sauce, thawed
  • 1/4 cupplain bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cupgrated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Heat 1 tbs of the oil in a medium skillet over medium heat until hot. Add tomatoes, garlic and green chilies. Cook 1 to 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally. Spread in bottom of ungreased 9 or 10 inch quiche dish or glass pie pan. Top with cauliflower in cheese sauce.
  2. In a small bowl, combine crumbs, Parmesan and remaining 2 tablespoons of oil. Mix well. Sprinkle over cauliflower mixture.
  3. Bake 20-22 minutes or until edges are bubbly and crumbs are golden.
