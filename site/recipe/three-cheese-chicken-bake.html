Three Cheese Chicken Bake

Photo: Vegan Feast Catering (CC BY 2.0)

A baked casserole layering noodles, diced cooked chicken and three cheeses (ricotta or cottage, cheddar and Parmesan) in a cream-of-chicken mushroom sauce, finished with buttered bread crumbs.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupchopped onions
  • 1/2 cupchopped green peppers
  • 3 tbsbutter or margarine
  • 1 can10 3/4 oz. cream of chicken soup, undiluted
  • 1 can8 oz
  • sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 jar2 oz. chopped pimientos, drained
  • 1/2 tspdried basil
  • 1 pkg8 oz noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cupsdiced cooked chicken
  • 2 cupsRicotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 cups8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cupgrated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cupbuttered bread crumbs

Directions

  1. In a skillet, saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender. Remove from the heat. Stir in the soup, mushrooms, pimientos and basil; set aside. In a large bowl, combine noodles, chicken and cheeses; add mushroom sauce and mix well. Transfer to a greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until bubbly. Sprinkle with crumbs. Bake 15 minutes longer. 12-15 servings
  1. In a skillet, saute the onion and green pepper in the butter until tender, then remove from the heat.
  2. Stir in the cream of chicken soup, mushrooms, pimientos and basil; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the noodles, chicken and the cheeses (ricotta or cottage, cheddar and Parmesan); add the mushroom sauce and mix well.
  4. Transfer to a greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish.
  5. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes or until bubbly.
  6. Sprinkle with the buttered bread crumbs and bake 15 minutes longer.

Goes well with

  • Sorrento Chicken Squisito
  • Cheesy Macaroni Divan
  • Baked Pasta
