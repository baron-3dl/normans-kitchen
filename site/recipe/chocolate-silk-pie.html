Chocolate Silk Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupbutter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 1 ozsquare unsweetened chocolate, melted
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 2eggs, divided
  • Sweetened whipped Cream
  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • 1 1/2 cupsgraham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tbspsugar
  • 1/3 cupbutter or margarine, melted

Directions

  1. Cream butter; gradually add sugar, beating at medium speed of an electric mixer until well blended. Stir in chocolate and vanilla. Add 1 egg, and eat 4 minutes; add remaining egg, and beat 5 minutes. Spoon mixture into graham cracker crust. Chill at lest 8 hours. Serve with a dollop of whipped cream. Yield: one 9 inch pie.
  2. Graham Cracker Crust
  3. Combine crumbs, sugar and melted butter. Mix well press mixture into 9 inch pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes. Cool. Fill with mixture from above, chill etc.
