Peaches and Cream Cheese Cake

Photo: SheGotTheBeat (CC BY 2.0)

A deep pie-pan dessert with a vanilla-pudding cake batter topped with sliced peaches and a cream cheese layer, finished with cinnamon sugar.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cupall purpose flour
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 pkg3.2 oz Dry Vanilla Pudding mix not instant
  • 3 tbsmargarine, softened
  • 1egg
  • 1/2 cupmilk
  • 1 can15-20 oz sliced peaches, well drained
  • 1 pkg8 oz cream cheese softened
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 3 tbsreserved juice
  • 1 tbssugar
  • 1/2 tspcinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottom and sides of 9 inch or 10 inch deep pie pan.
  2. Combine first 7 ingredients in large mixing bowl, beat for 2 minutes at medium speed, pour into pie pan.
  3. Place can of well drained peaches or pineapple over batter.
  4. Combine cream cheese,sugar and 3 tbs reserved juice in a bowl. Beat at medium speed for 2 minutes. Spoon to within 1 inch of edge of batter.
  5. Combine sugar and cinnamon to sprinkle over cheese filling.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes until crust is golden brown. Filling will appear soft. Store in refrigerator.
