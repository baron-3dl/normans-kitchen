Big Brownie Oatmeal Drops

Chewy drop cookies made from a boxed brownie mix stirred together with oats and chopped walnuts, with crunchy edges and soft centers.

Ingredients

  • 1 family sizebrownie mix with syrup pouch 1 lb.
  • prepare as directed using 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cupsuncooked oats regular or quick
  • 1 cupwalnuts, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheets. Mix all ingredients until well blended. Cover and let stand 30 minutes for oats to absorb liquid. Drop rounded tbsp 1 1/2 inch apart on cookie sheets. Bake 10 to 12 minutes until tops look crackled and satiny. Remove with spatula to a wire rack to cool. Makes about 32 cookies. Edges are crunchy centers are soft.
  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheets.
  2. Prepare the family-size (1 lb.) brownie mix with syrup pouch as directed, using 2 eggs. Mix in the oats and walnuts until well blended.
  3. Cover and let stand 30 minutes for the oats to absorb the liquid.
  4. Drop rounded tablespoons 1 1/2 inches apart onto the cookie sheets.
  5. Bake 10 to 12 minutes, until the tops look crackled and satiny.
  6. Remove with a spatula to a wire rack to cool. Makes about 32 cookies; edges are crunchy, centers are soft.

Goes well with

  • Brownies
  • Peanut Butter and Honey Oatmeal Cookies
  • Cheese Brownies
