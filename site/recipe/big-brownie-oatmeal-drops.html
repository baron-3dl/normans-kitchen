Big Brownie Oatmeal Drops

Ingredients

  • 1 family sizebrownie mix with syrup pouch 1 lb.
  • prepare as directed using 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cupsuncooked oats regular or quick
  • 1 cupwalnuts, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheets. Mix all ingredients until well blended. Cover and let stand 30 minutes for oats to absorb liquid. Drop rounded tbsp 1 1/2 inch apart on cookie sheets. Bake 10 to 12 minutes until tops look crackled and satiny. Remove with spatula to a wire rack to cool. Makes about 32 cookies. Edges are crunchy centers are soft.
