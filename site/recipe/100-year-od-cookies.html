100 Year Od Cookies

Ingredients

  • 4eggs
  • 4 cupssugar
  • 1 1/2 lbsMargarine
  • 2 tspbaking soda
  • 1 cupsour cream
  • 2 cupsnuts
  • 1 lbraisins
  • 9-10 cupsflour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients together thoroughly. Chill dough. Bake on greased cookie sheet.
