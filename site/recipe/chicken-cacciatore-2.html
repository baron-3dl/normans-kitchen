Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients

  • Chicken cut in pieces
  • clovegarlic
  • 1/4 tsporegano
  • 1 cansm mushrooms, drained
  • 1 tbsWorcestershire sauce
  • dashTabasco
  • 2green pepper diced
  • 1onion, chopped
  • 2tomatoes, Jullienned
  • 1/4 cupcooking wine
  • 2 canstomato sauce
  • 1 lbspaghetti

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Mix oregano to flour, roll chicken in flour. Fry in oil until done. Add garlic while chicken is frying. Skin up on chicken. When chicken is done add wine, vegetables, mushroom, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco, little salt and pepper and tomato sauce. Cover and cook about 15 minutes on low heat. Serve over spaghetti
