E-Z 3 Step Cheese Cake

A simple baked cheesecake of cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and semi-sweet chocolate chips in a graham cracker crust, topped with chocolate shavings.

Ingredients

  • 2 8 ozpkg cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 2eggs
  • 1/2 cupsemi sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Beat cheese, sugar, vanilla until well blended. Add eggs one at a time blend and mix well. Stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Pour mixture into ready made Graham cracker pie crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate for 3 hours. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings.

  2. Add the eggs one at a time, blending and mixing well after each. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  3. Pour the mixture into a ready-made graham cracker pie crust. Bake at 350°F for 40 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
  4. Cool, then refrigerate for 3 hours. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings.

Goes well with

  • Cheesecake
  • Individual Cheese Cakes
  • Sour Cream Cheesecake
  • Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake
