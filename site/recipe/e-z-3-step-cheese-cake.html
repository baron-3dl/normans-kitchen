E-Z 3 Step Cheese Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 8 ozpkg cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 2eggs
  • 1/2 cupsemi sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Beat cheese, sugar, vanilla until well blended. Add eggs one at a time blend and mix well. Stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Pour mixture into ready made Graham cracker pie crust. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate for 3 hours. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings.
