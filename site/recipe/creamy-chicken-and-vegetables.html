Creamy Chicken and Vegetables

Serves 4

Photo: pathlost (CC BY 2.0)

A one-skillet main of chicken breasts browned then simmered with low-fat cream of mushroom soup, milk, lemon juice, basil, and frozen vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4boneless chicken brest halves
  • 1/8 tspgarlic powder
  • 1 can10 3/4 oz. Low Fat Cr of Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cupmilk
  • 1 tbsplemon juice
  • 1/4 tspdried basil leaves (crushed)
  • 1 bag16 oz. frozen vegetable combination

Directions

  1. Sprinkle chicken with garlic powder.
  2. Spray skillet with vegetable cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat 1 minute. Cook chicken 10 min. or until browned. Set chicken aside.
  3. Add soup, milk, lemon juice, basil and vegetables. Heat to boil. Return chicken to pan. Cover and cook over low heat 10 min. or until chicken is done. serves 4
