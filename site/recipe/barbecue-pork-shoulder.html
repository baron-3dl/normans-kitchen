Barbecue Pork Shoulder

Photo: benuski (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Pork shoulder simmered until tender, sliced, and baked under a ketchup and brown sugar spice sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbspork shoulder
  • 1 cupcatsup
  • 1/2 cupbrown sugar
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 1 tsppepper
  • 1-2 tspchili powder
  • 1/4 cup+ 2 tbs vinegar
  • 2 tbsplemon juice
  • 1/4 cupWorcestershire sauce
  • 2 tspprepared mustard

Directions

  1. Cover meat with slightly salted water and cook over medium heat 2-2 1/2 hours till tender. Drain. Slice into thin slices place in 2 quart baking dish. Combine all the spice ingredients and spoon over pork slices. Bake at 300 degrees for 45 minutes. Turn meat occasionally.
  1. Cover the meat with slightly salted water and cook over medium heat 2 to 2 1/2 hours, until tender.
  2. Drain.
  3. Slice into thin slices and place in a 2-quart baking dish.
  4. Combine all the spice ingredients and spoon over the pork slices.
  5. Bake at 300 degrees for 45 minutes, turning the meat occasionally.

Goes well with

  • Bar-B-Q Pork Roast
  • Pulled Pork
  • Carolina Barbecued Pork
  • Pork Barbecue on Buns
