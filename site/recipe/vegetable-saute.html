Vegetable Saute

Ingredients

  • 1 clovegarlic
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1/2 cupred/green peppers, celery
  • 1/4 tsporegano, crushed ice
  • 1 can16 oz. green beans
  • 1 can8 oz. sliced carrots

Directions

  1. Heat: Garlic in oil. Add peppers, celery, and oregano. Cook til tender, add green beans, carrots heat till done.
