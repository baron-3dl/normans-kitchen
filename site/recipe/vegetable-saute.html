Vegetable Saute

Photo: [puamelia] (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A quick skillet side of peppers, celery, green beans, and carrots sauteed with garlic and oregano.

Ingredients

  • 1 clovegarlic
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 1/2 cupred/green peppers, celery
  • 1/4 tsporegano, crushed ice
  • 1 can16 oz. green beans
  • 1 can8 oz. sliced carrots

Directions

  1. Heat: Garlic in oil. Add peppers, celery, and oregano. Cook til tender, add green beans, carrots heat till done.
  1. Heat the garlic in the oil.
  2. Add the peppers, celery, and oregano and cook until tender.
  3. Add the green beans and carrots and heat until done.

Goes well with

  • Salsa Green Beans
  • Zesty Carrots
