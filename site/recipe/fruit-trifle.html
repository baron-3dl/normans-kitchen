Fruit Trifle

Ingredients

  • 1 pinthalf and half cream
  • 1 pkginstant vanilla pudding
  • 1 pintstrawberries
  • 1 cupcubed cantaloupe
  • 2 medpeaches, sliced and dripped in orange juice
  • 1 cupcultivated blueberries
  • 2 cupsangel food cake (cubed)
  • 2 medbananas
  • orange liqueur

Directions

  1. In medium bowl pour half and half and pudding mix, gently beat about 2 minutes. Cover and refrigerate.
  2. Reserve several strawberry slices. Mix strawberries, cantaloupe, peaches and blueberries. Cover and refrigerate.
  3. Assemble shortly before you serve in a large glass bowl.
  4. Cake first, fruit mixture and pudding mixture, do this twice, just before serving peel bananas and slice sprinkle with liqueur. Arrange around edge and put extra strawberries on the center. Serve at once.
