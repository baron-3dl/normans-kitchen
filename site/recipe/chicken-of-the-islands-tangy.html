Chicken of the Islands Tangy

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupvinegar
  • 1/2 cupsoy sauce
  • 2-3 lbs.chicken
  • 1 clovegarlic minced
  • dashfresh pepper

Directions

  1. Put vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, pepper in skillet, add chicken, let marinate for 30 minutes, turn once or twice. Heat to boil on top of stove. Cover, lower heat to simmer about 40 minutes. Marinade will be absorbed.
