Maple Glazed Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lbspork tenderloin
  • 3/4 cuppure maple syrup
  • 2 tbspchili sauce
  • 2 tbspsoy sauce
  • 1 tbspunsweetened frozen orange juice, thawed
  • 1 tbspDijon mustard
  • 1 tbspWorcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbspcurry powder
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 1 tsptoasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Place pork in an 11 by 7 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the maple syrup, chili sauce, soy sauce, orange juice concentrate, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, curry powder and garlic. Pour over the pork.
  3. Bake for about 30 to 40 minutes, or until a meat thermometer inserted into the tenderloin reads 150
  4. Remove from oven and allow to stand for 10 minutes before slicing into 1/4 inch slices; spoon the sauce on top and sprinkle with sesame seeds
