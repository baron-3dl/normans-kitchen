Black Bottom Goodies

Photo: Gary Soup (CC BY 2.0)

Miniature chocolate cupcakes made from an eggless cocoa batter, each filled with a sweetened cream cheese and chocolate chip mixture and baked in mini muffin pans. Makes about 5 dozen.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupall purpose flour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1/4 cupcocoa
  • 1 cupwater
  • 1/3 cupvegetable oil
  • 1 tbspvinegar
  • 1 tbspvanilla
  • Filling
  • 1 8 oz.cream cheese, softened
  • 1egg
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/8 tspsalt
  • 1 6 oz.pkg semisweet choc tidbits

Directions

  1. Combine frist 5 ingredients in large bowl, make well in center. Combine water, oil, vinegar and vanilla, add to dry ingredients, stirring well. Spoon batter into paper lined miniature muffin pans 2/3 full.
  2. FILLING
  3. Combine cream cheese and next 3 ingredients. Stirring well. Stir in chocolate chips. Spoon 1 teaspoon mixture into each. Bake at 350 degrees 10-15 min. Makes approx 5 dozen
  1. Combine the first 5 ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt, sugar, and cocoa) in a large bowl; make a well in the center.
  2. Combine the water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla; add to the dry ingredients, stirring well.
  3. Spoon the batter into paper-lined miniature muffin pans, filling each 2/3 full.
  4. For the filling: combine the cream cheese, egg, sugar, and salt, stirring well. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Spoon 1 teaspoon of the filling mixture into each muffin cup.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes. Makes approximately 5 dozen.
