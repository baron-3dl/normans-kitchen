Strawberry French Toast

Photo: stu_spivack (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A kid-friendly French toast made by dipping cream cheese and strawberry jam sandwiches in an egg-and-milk batter and frying until golden.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupwhipped cream cheese
  • 12slices bread
  • Strawberry jam
  • 3 tbspmilk
  • 1/8 tspsalt
  • 3eggs

Directions

  1. Spread cream cheese and strawberry jam on bread making sandwich.
  2. Mix eggs, milk, salt. Dip sandwich in mixture, both sides. Fry both sides until done.
  1. Spread the whipped cream cheese and strawberry jam on the bread to make sandwiches.
  2. In a bowl, mix the eggs, milk, and salt.
  3. Dip each sandwich in the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Fry both sides until done.
