Buttermilk Pie

Photo: ralph and jenny (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cupbutter or margarine, softened
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 3eggs
  • 3 tbspall purpose flour
  • dashground nutmeg
  • 1 cupbuttermilk
  • 1unbaked 9-inch pastry shell

Directions

  1. Cream butter; gradually add sugar, beating at medium speed of an electric mixer. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Add flour and nutmeg, beating at low speed until blended. Add buttermilk, beating well. Pour into pastry shell; bake at 325 degrees for 55 minutes or until pie is set. Cool on wire rack. Yield: one 9-inch pie.
