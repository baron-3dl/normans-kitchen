Greens with Bacon Dressing

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 Cupssalad greens (spinach, romaine, lettuce)
  • 4green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cupgreen pepper, chopped
  • 1 or 2hard cooked eggs, chopped
  • 2 to 3 tbsbrown sugar
  • 2 tbsred wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsWorcestershire sauce
  • 1 to 2 tbslemon juice
  • 1/4 tspgarlic salt
  • 8 slicesbacon

Directions

  1. Place greens in a salad bowl. Add onions and green pepper. Cut bacon into small pieces and fry bacon until crisp. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  2. Le bacon fat cool slightly. Add brown sugar and stir well. Add vinegar, Worcestershire, lemon juice and garlic salt. Stir and pour over the greens. Toss gently. Garnish with bacon pieces and hard cooked eggs. Makes 6 servings.
