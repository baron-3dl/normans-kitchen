Oven Fried Potatoes

Photo: Gudlyf (CC BY 2.0)

Potato wedges baked in a single layer and basted with a seasoned mixture of oil, parmesan, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 3 medpotatoes
  • 1/4 cupvegetable oil
  • 1 tbspparmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tspgarlic powder
  • 1/4 tsppaprika
  • 1/4 tsppepper

Directions

  1. Clean potatoes, cut into wedges, lay in 13 x 9 x 2 pan in single layer and brush with mixture of above ingredients. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes or so. Basting occasionally with mixture.
  1. Clean the 3 medium potatoes and cut them into wedges.
  2. Lay the wedges in a single layer in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan.
  3. Combine the 1/4 cup vegetable oil, 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon paprika, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper; brush the potatoes with this mixture.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes or so, basting occasionally with the mixture.

Goes well with

  • Oven French Fries
  • Parmesan Potato Wedges
  • Portuguese Oven-Roasted Potatoes
