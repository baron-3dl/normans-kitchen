Chocolate Fudge Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 lgeeggs
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cupbutter or margarine, melted
  • 1/3 cupunsweetened cocoa
  • 1/4 cupall purpose flour
  • 1/8 tspsalt
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 3/4 cupchopped pecans

Directions

  1. Whisk together eggs and sugar in a large bowl; add butter and next 4 ingredients, stirring until blended. Stir in pecans and spoon into a lightly greased 9 inch pie plate.
  2. Bake a 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes. (Mixture will rise, then fall as it cools)
