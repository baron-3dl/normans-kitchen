Oven French Fries

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupparmesan cheese
  • 2 tsporegano
  • 2 bakingpotatoes w/skins
  • 1egg white (beaten)
  • vegetable spray

Directions

  1. Mix cheese and oregano. Slice potato for French fries. Coat with egg white, dredge in cheese. Spray cookie sheet and bake 25 minutes at 450 degrees.
