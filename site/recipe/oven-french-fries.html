Oven French Fries

Photo: waynewalterberry (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Oven-baked French fries made from skin-on baking potatoes coated in beaten egg white and a parmesan-oregano crust.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupparmesan cheese
  • 2 tsporegano
  • 2 bakingpotatoes w/skins
  • 1egg white (beaten)
  • vegetable spray

Directions

  1. Mix cheese and oregano. Slice potato for French fries. Coat with egg white, dredge in cheese. Spray cookie sheet and bake 25 minutes at 450 degrees.
  1. Mix the 1/2 cup parmesan cheese with the 2 teaspoons oregano.
  2. Slice the 2 baking potatoes (with skins) into French fries.
  3. Coat the potato slices with the beaten egg white, then dredge them in the cheese mixture.
  4. Spray a cookie sheet with vegetable spray and arrange the potatoes on it.
  5. Bake at 450 degrees for 25 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Parmesan Potato Wedges
  • Oven Fried Potatoes
