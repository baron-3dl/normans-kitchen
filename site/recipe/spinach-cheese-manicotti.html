Spinach Cheese Manicotti

Ingredients

  • 1 medonion, finely chopped
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsvegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cupsricotta cheese
  • 1 cup4 oz shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 ozcream cheese, softened
  • 6 tbsgrated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 tspItalian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1 pkg10 oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed
  • 8manicotti shells, cooked and drained
  • 1 jar26 oz spaghetti sauce

Directions

  1. In a small skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil for 3 minutes; set aside. In a mixing bowl combine the ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella, cream cheese, 4 tbs Parmesan, Italian seasoning and pepper; beat until smooth. Stir in onion mixture, add the chopped spinach, mix well then stuff the manicotti shells, place in Baking dish cover with sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Bake at 350 degrees till done.
