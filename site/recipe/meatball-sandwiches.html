Meatball Sandwiches

Makes 6 servings

Photo: ginnerobot (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1 batchMeatballs frozen or thawed
  • 1 cupketchup
  • 3/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 1/4 1/2chopped onions
  • 1/4 tspgarlic powder
  • 6sandwich rolls, split

Directions

  1. Place meatballs in an ungreased 1-qt baking dish. Combine the next five ingredients; pour over meatballs. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Serve on rolls. Yield: 6 servings.
  2. These meatballs may be served as appetizers with toothpicks instead of rolls.
