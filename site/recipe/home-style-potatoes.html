Home Style Potatoes

Makes 6-8 servings

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Sliced potatoes baked in a seasoned mixture of butter, onion soup mix, and rosemary.

Ingredients

  • 6 medpotatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 3/4 cupwater
  • 1/2 cupbutter or margarine
  • 1 envonion soup mix
  • 1/2 tspdried rosemary, crushed

Directions

  1. Placer potatoes in a greased 2 quart baking dish. In a saucepan, combine water, butter, soup mix and rosemary; heat until butter is melted. Pour over potatoes. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes. Uncover; bake 20 minutes longer or until the potatoes are tender.
  1. Place the potatoes in a greased 2-quart baking dish.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the water, butter, soup mix, and rosemary; heat until the butter is melted. Pour over the potatoes.
  3. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
  4. Uncover and bake 20 minutes longer, or until the potatoes are tender.

Goes well with

  • Portuguese Oven-Roasted Potatoes
  • Oven Fried Potatoes
  • Parmesan Potato Wedges
  • Stovetop Scalloped Potatoes
