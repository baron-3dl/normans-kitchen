Green Beans with Feta

Photo: Average Jane (CC BY 2.0)

A warm skillet side of green beans cooked with garlic, Italian seasoning, and plum tomatoes, then topped with crumbled Feta cheese and toasted pine nuts.

Ingredients

  • 2 pkgfrozen green beans, thawed drained
  • 1 tspolive oil
  • 2 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 2 tspdried Italian seasoning
  • 4plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbslemon juice
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tspground pepper
  • 4 oz pkgcrumbled Feta cheese
  • 1/4 cuptoasted pine nuts

Directions

  1. Press beans between paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  2. In a large nonstick skillet, heat oil until hot. Cook garlic and Italian seasoning over medium heat 1 minute; add green beans and cook 5-7 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and cook, stirring constantly, 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Stir in lemon juice, salt and pepper. Sprinkle with cheese and pine nuts.
  3. Toast pine nuts in a 350 degree oven for 6-8 minutes.
  1. Toast the pine nuts in a 350°F oven for 6-8 minutes.
  2. Press the thawed, drained green beans between paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. In a large nonstick skillet, heat the olive oil until hot. Cook the garlic and Italian seasoning over medium heat for 1 minute.
  4. Add the green beans and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in the chopped plum tomatoes and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
  6. Stir in the lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  7. Sprinkle with the crumbled Feta cheese and toasted pine nuts.

Goes well with

  • Salsa Green Beans
  • Tossed Greek Salad
