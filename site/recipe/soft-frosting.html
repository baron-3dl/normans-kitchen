Soft Frosting

Photo: looktothe_stars (CC BY 2.0)

A cooked-flour (ermine-style) frosting made by thickening milk with Wondra flour, then beating it into creamed butter, Crisco, and sugar with vanilla until light like whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cupmilk
  • 5 tbsWondra Flour
  • 1/2 cupbutter (only)
  • 1 cupcrisco
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Put cup of milk in pan over low heat, add wondra flour stirring until sauce thickens. Set aside to cool.
  2. Cream butter, crisco an sugar. Blend well. Add milk mixture,beat until well mixed. Add vanilla. Continue beating until like whipped cream.
  3. Refrigerate until needed.
  1. Put the cup of milk in a pan over low heat, add the Wondra flour, and stir until the sauce thickens. Set aside to cool.
  2. Cream the butter, Crisco, and sugar; blend well.
  3. Add the cooled milk mixture and beat until well mixed.
  4. Add the vanilla and continue beating until it is like whipped cream.
  5. Refrigerate until needed.

Goes well with

  • Too Good Frosting
  • Fluffy Chocolate Frosting
  • Creamy Chocolate Frosting
  • Fat Free Frosting
