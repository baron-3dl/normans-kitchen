Special Rice Dessert (make a day ahead)

Ingredients

  • 1 cuprice
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1 cancrushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 pintwhipping cream
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 jarcherries, drained

Directions

  1. Put rice and sugar in saucepan with water, (plenty of it) boil until cooked. Fold in pineapple and refrigerate until next day.
  2. Whip cream with vanilla, fold cream into rice, fold in cherries.
