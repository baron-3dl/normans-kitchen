Chicken Cordon Bleu

Ingredients

  • 4 pcschicken breasts
  • 4ham slices
  • 4Swiss cheese slices
  • 1/4 cupflour
  • 1/4 cuphoney mustard
  • 1/2 cupseasoned bread crumbs (Italian style)
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat baking sheet with olive oil or cooking spray.
  2. Pound chicken pieces, put one slice of ham and cheese on each breast
  3. Roll each breast jellyroll style tucking ends in and securing with toothpicks. Place flour, mustard and crumbs in separate shallow bowls, add chicken to flour and coat, then to mustard to coat and then place in bread crumbs and coat well.
  4. Add to baking sheet and coat with cooking spry. Bake until chicken is golden and cooked through about 40 minutes, remove toothpicks and serve.
