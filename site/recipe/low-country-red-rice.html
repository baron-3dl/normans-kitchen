Low Country Red Rice

Photo: young shanahan (CC BY 2.0)

A Southern Low Country tomato rice cooked in bacon grease with onion and crushed tomatoes, seasoned with black pepper and cayenne, and topped with crumbled bacon.

Ingredients

  • 3 slicesthick cut bacon
  • 1 smonion, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cuplong rain rice
  • 1 cupcrushed tomatoes, undrained
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • Cayenne to taste
  • 1 cupcup chicken stock, broth or water

Directions

  1. In a heavy saucepan, fry the bacon until it is crisp. Remove bacon from the pan and drain on a paper towel. When cool, crumble bacon.
  2. Pour off the excess bacon grease, leaving only enough to coat bottom of the pan. Add the chopped onion and saute until it softens, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the rice and saute until the grains are coated with the bacon grease, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, salt, black pepper and cayenne pepper. Stir, add stock, both or water, stir again and cover pan. Cook mixture over low heat for 30 minutes without stiring.
  5. Transfer the hot rice to a bowl and garnish with the crumbled bacon.
