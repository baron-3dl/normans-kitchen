Tangy Baked Pork Chops

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cupswater
  • 1lemon (juice and grated rind)
  • 1onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/4 cupWorcestershire sauce
  • dashTabasco
  • 2 ribscelery, chopped
  • 1 spchili powder
  • 1/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 1 cupketchup
  • 1/4 cupvinegar
  • 1 1/2-2 lbspork chops (or can use chicken)

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients, except pork or chicken, in a 2 quart saucepan. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. Brown chops or chicken; place in a baking dish. Cover with sauce; bake for 1 1/2 to 2 hours at 350 degrees. Serves 6.
