Aunt Suzi's Brisket

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cupdark brown sugar
  • 3/4 cupchili sauce
  • 3/4 cupketchup
  • 3/4 envdry onion soup mixture
  • 12 oz.bottle beer
  • 3-4 lbsbeef brisket
  • 15 smred potatoes
  • 1 1/2 lbsbaby carrots

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees. Mix first 4 ingredients; stir in beer.
  2. Place brisket, fat side up, in a roasting pan; pierce with a fork,. Arrange potatoes and carrots evenly around brisket; pour beer mixture evenly over brisket, potatoes and carrots.
  3. Bake, covered, at 325 degrees for 3 12/ hours, basting occasionally with sauce. Remove from oven and let brisket stand in pan, uncovered, 20 minutes. remove brisket from pan, and cut into slices. Return slices to pan with potatoes and carrots.
  4. Bake, covered, 30 minutes or until tender.
