Peanut Butter Choc. Chip Cookies

Photo: SanFranAnnie (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A flourless four-ingredient cookie of peanut butter, brown sugar and baking soda with chocolate chips stirred in.

Ingredients

  • 1 cupPeanut Butter
  • 1 cupbrown sugar, packed
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 cupchocolate chips

Directions

  1. Blend first three ingredients, then stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
