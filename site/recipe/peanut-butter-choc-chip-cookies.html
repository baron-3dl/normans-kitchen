Peanut Butter Choc. Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cupPeanut Butter
  • 1 cupbrown sugar, packed
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 cupchocolate chips

Directions

  1. Blend first three ingredients, then stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.
