Individual Cheese Cakes

Photo: Breville USA (CC BY 2.0)

Miniature cheesecakes made from a cream cheese batter baked in cupcake tins over vanilla wafers and topped with fruit.

Ingredients

  • 2 pkg8 oz cream cheese
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Mix al together and pour over vanilla wafers in cupcake tins.
  2. Bake at 325 degrees 15 minutes. Cool, add spoonful of any fruit topping.
