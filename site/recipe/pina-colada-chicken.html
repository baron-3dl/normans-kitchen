Pina Colada Chicken

Photo: Bernard DUPONT (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Chicken thighs marinated in a pina colada mixture of pineapple juice, rum, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic, then baked and topped with pineapple slices and cherries.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (8 oz.)Sliced Pineapples
  • 1 envPina Colada Mix
  • 1/4 cuprum
  • 1/4 cupsoy sauce
  • 1 tspGinger ale
  • 1 clovegarlic minced
  • 8chicken thighs
  • 8cherries

Directions

  1. Drain pineapples, save juice, set pineapple aside. Combine juice and next 5 ingredients. Place chicken in baking dish 12 x 8 x 2 lightly greased. Pour juice mixture over chicken, marinate 1 hour. Bake uncovered 400 degrees for 30 minutes, reduce heat to 350 for 20 minutes, top with slices of pineapples and cherries. Bake 5 minutes more.
  1. Drain the pineapples, saving the juice; set the pineapple aside.
  2. Combine the reserved juice with the next 5 ingredients: the pina colada mix, rum, soy sauce, ginger ale, and minced garlic.
  3. Place the chicken thighs in a lightly greased 12 x 8 x 2 baking dish.
  4. Pour the juice mixture over the chicken and marinate 1 hour.
  5. Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake 20 minutes.
  7. Top with the pineapple slices and cherries.
  8. Bake 5 minutes more.

Goes well with

  • Sweet and Sour Pork
