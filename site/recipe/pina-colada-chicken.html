Pina Colada Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (8 oz.)Sliced Pineapples
  • 1 envPina Colada Mix
  • 1/4 cuprum
  • 1/4 cupsoy sauce
  • 1 tspGinger ale
  • 1 clovegarlic minced
  • 8chicken thighs
  • 8cherries

Directions

  1. Drain pineapples, save juice, set pineapple aside. Combine juice and next 5 ingredients. Place chicken in baking dish 12 x 8 x 2 lightly greased. Pour juice mixture over chicken, marinate 1 hour. Bake uncovered 400 degrees for 30 minutes, reduce heat to 350 for 20 minutes, top with slices of pineapples and cherries. Bake 5 minutes more.
