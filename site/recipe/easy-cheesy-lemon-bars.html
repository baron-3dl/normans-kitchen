Easy Cheesy Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgPillsbury Lemon Plus Cake Mix
  • 1/3 cupmargarine
  • 1egg
  • Filling
  • 1 canReady to Spread Lemon Frosting
  • 1 pkg8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1egg
  • 1/2 cupchopped nuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven top 350 degrees. Grease and flour pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all base ingredients at low speed until crumbly, press in pan.
  3. In small bowl combine frosting, cream cheese and egg a low speed until well blended, beat at highest speed until smooth.
  4. Stir in nuts, spread over base.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees 40 to 50 minutes or until firm.
  6. Chill 2 hours before serving. Cut into bars.
