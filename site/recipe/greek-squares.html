Greek Squares

Photo: moria (CC BY 2.0)

A magic-cookie-style bar of graham cracker crumbs, nuts, chocolate chips, and coconut bound with condensed milk and baked over a butter-lined pan.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 stickbutter melted in bottom of pan
  • 1 pkggraham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cupchopped nuts
  • 1 smpkg semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cupshredded coconut
  • 1 cupcondensed milk

Directions

  1. Mix in bowl and put in 8 x 9 pan 350 degrees for 30 minutes
  1. Melt 1/2 stick of butter in the bottom of the pan.
  2. Mix the graham cracker crumbs, chopped nuts, chocolate chips, coconut, and condensed milk together in a bowl.
  3. Put the mixture in an 8 x 9 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Heavenly Hash
