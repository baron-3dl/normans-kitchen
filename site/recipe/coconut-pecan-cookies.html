Coconut Pecan Cookies

Makes 6 1/2 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • Topping
  • 1egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 can5 oz evaporated milk
  • 2/3 cupsugar
  • 1/4 cupbutter (no substitutes) cubed
  • 1 1/3 cupsflaked coconut
  • 1/2 cupchopped pecans
  • COOKIE DOUGH
  • 1 cupbutter, softened
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cupsall purpose flour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 4 cups24 oz semi sweet chocolate chips divided
  • 1/4 cupflaked coconut

Directions

  1. For frosting, in a saucepan, combine the egg, milk, sugar and butter. Cook and stir over medium-low heat for 10-12 minutes or until slightly thickened and mixture reaches 160 degrees. Stir in coconut and pecans. Set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in 2 cups chips and coconut. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 inch apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
  3. In a microwave, melt the remaining chocolate chips; stir until smooth. Frost cooled cookies, then drizzle with melted chocolate,
