Coconut Pecan Cookies

Makes 6 1/2 dozen cookies

Photo: Vegan Feast Catering (CC BY 2.0)

Chocolate chip and coconut drop cookies topped with a cooked coconut-pecan frosting and a drizzle of melted chocolate. Makes 6 1/2 dozen cookies.

Ingredients

  • Topping
  • 1egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 can5 oz evaporated milk
  • 2/3 cupsugar
  • 1/4 cupbutter (no substitutes) cubed
  • 1 1/3 cupsflaked coconut
  • 1/2 cupchopped pecans
  • COOKIE DOUGH
  • 1 cupbutter, softened
  • 3/4 cupsugar
  • 3/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cupsall purpose flour
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 4 cups24 oz semi sweet chocolate chips divided
  • 1/4 cupflaked coconut

Directions

  1. For frosting, in a saucepan, combine the egg, milk, sugar and butter. Cook and stir over medium-low heat for 10-12 minutes or until slightly thickened and mixture reaches 160 degrees. Stir in coconut and pecans. Set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in 2 cups chips and coconut. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 inch apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
  3. In a microwave, melt the remaining chocolate chips; stir until smooth. Frost cooled cookies, then drizzle with melted chocolate,
  1. Make the frosting: in a saucepan, combine the egg, evaporated milk, sugar, and butter. Cook and stir over medium-low heat for 10-12 minutes, until slightly thickened and the mixture reaches 160 degrees.
  2. Stir in the coconut and pecans; set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, cream the butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture. Stir in 2 cups of the chocolate chips and the 1/4 cup coconut.
  5. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes, until lightly browned. Cool 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. In a microwave, melt the remaining chocolate chips; stir until smooth.
  8. Frost the cooled cookies with the coconut-pecan frosting, then drizzle with the melted chocolate.

Goes well with

  • Chocolate Bit Cookies
  • Macaroon Cookies
  • Coconut Custard Pie
