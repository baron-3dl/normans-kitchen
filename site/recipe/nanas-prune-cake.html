Nana's Prune Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cupsflour tortillas
  • 1 tspbaking soda
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1 tbspcinnamon, nutmeg, allspice
  • 1 cupcorn oil
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 3eggs
  • 1 cupprunes, pitted and cut in pieces
  • 1 cupbuttermilk
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • PRUNE CAKE GLAZE
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cupbuttermilk
  • 1/2 stickbutter
  • 1/4 cupcorn syrup
  • 1/2 tbsbaking soda
  • 1/2 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, salt, baking soda and spices. Set aside. In a large mixing bowl beat together the corn oil and sugar, add eggs, one at time beating well after each addition, stir in vanilla. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with buttermilk about 1/3 at a time, blending well after each addition, stir in prunes and nuts. Pour into greased and floured tube or bundt pan. Bake in oven at 350 degrees until cake tester comes out clean, about and hour. About 15 minutes before cake is done start preparing glaze.
  2. GLAZE
  3. In a 3 quart sauce pan put all ingredients in and cook up to a boil for 10 minutes. Stirring occasionally. When the cake is done and removed from oven . . pour warm glaze over the cake in the pan allow to soak into cake and cool completely before removing from pan.
