Peanut Butter Dessert

Makes 15 servings

Ingredients

  • 35cream filled chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed
  • 1/3 cupbutter or margarine, melted
  • 1 5.1 ozPkg vanilla instant pudding mix
  • 2 cupsmilk
  • 1 1/4 cupscreamy peanut butter
  • 1 8 ozfrozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions

  1. Combine cookie crumbs and butter, and reserve 1/4 crumb mixture. Press into bottom of a 13 x 9 2 inch pan. Bake at 375 degrees for 5 minutes. Let cool on a wire rack for 15 minutes.
  2. Combine pudding mix and milk; beat with wire whisk until smooth. Add peanut butter, beating with whisk until smooth and thickened. Fold in whipped topping. Spoon over crumb crust, and sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture. Cover and chill at least 3 hours.
