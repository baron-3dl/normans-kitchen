Easy Cabbage and Beef Rolls

Makes 10 rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 lgecabbages
  • 1 can15 oz. tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 lbground beef
  • 1 can4 oz mushroom stems pieces
  • 1/2 cupuncooked instant rice
  • 1/2 cupchopped onion
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tsppepper
  • 1/8 tspgarlic powder
  • 1 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tsplemon juice
  • 1 tbspcornstarch
  • 1 tbspwater

Directions

  1. Remove 10 outer leaves of cabbage. Cover leaves with boiling water; cover and let stand 10 minutes. Drain well.
  2. Combine 1/2 cup tomato sauce and next 7 ingredients, mixing well. Place 1/3 cup of meat mixture in center of each cabbage leaf. Fold 2 opposite ends over, and place rolls, seam side down, in a lightly greased 8-inch baking dish. Combine remaining tomato sauce, sugar, and lemon juice; pour over cabbage rolls. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  3. Remove cabbage rolls to a serving platter; drain tomato sauce mixture into a saucepan. Combine cornstarch and water, stirring until smooth; stir into tomato sauce mixture. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Boil 1 minute. Pour over cabbage rolls.
