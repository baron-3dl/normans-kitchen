Pie Burger

Photo: amberdegrace (CC BY 2.0)

A savory ground-beef pie: seasoned beef with onion soup mix, pickle relish, and chili sauce baked in pie dough.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Brown lightly: 1 lb ground beef with 1 pkg dry onion soup mix and 1/2 tsp salt in skillet. Add 1/3 cup pickle relish or finely chopped cucumber pickle and 2 to 4 tbs chili sauce. Set aside. Make pie dough and add beef filling.
  1. In a skillet, lightly brown 1 lb ground beef with 1 package dry onion soup mix and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  2. Add 1/3 cup pickle relish (or finely chopped cucumber pickle) and 2 to 4 tablespoons chili sauce. Set aside.
  3. Make pie dough and add the beef filling.

Goes well with

  • Onion Layered Hamburger
  • Cheesy Potato Meat Pie
  • Soup Meat 'n Potatoes Pie
  • Cottage Pie
