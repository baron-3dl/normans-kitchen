Chocolate Chip Bars

Photo: moria (CC BY 2.0)

Quick bars made from a boxed cake mix with oil, water, eggs, and semi-sweet chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 boxwhite or yellow cake mix
  • 1/2 cupcooking oil
  • 2 tbswater
  • 2eggs
  • 1 cup6 oz. pkg semi-sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl till well blended. Transfer to ungreased 8 x 8 or 9 x 9 pan. Cook about 40 minutes or until cake is done. Cut into bars when cooled.
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well blended.
  3. Transfer to an ungreased 8 x 8 or 9 x 9 pan.
  4. Bake about 40 minutes, or until the cake is done.
  5. Cut into bars when cooled.

Goes well with

  • Chocolate Chip Squares
  • Berry Easy Dump Cake
  • Chocolate Bit Cookies
