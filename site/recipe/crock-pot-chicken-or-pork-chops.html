Crock Pot Chicken or Pork Chops

A slow-cooker dish in which chicken breasts (or pork chops) cook with chicken broth, cream soup, and Stove Top stuffing mix.

Ingredients

  • 4chicken breasts
  • 1 canfat free chicken broth
  • 1 cancream soup (any flavor)
  • 1 boxStove Top stuffing mix

Directions

  1. Put chicken in pot (frozen or thawed) pour 1 can chicken broth over them. Mix 1/2 cup water, cram soup and stuffing mix. Spoon over chicken.
  1. Put the chicken in the pot (frozen or thawed) and pour 1 can of chicken broth over them.
  2. Mix 1/2 cup water, the cream soup (any flavor), and the stuffing mix.
  3. Spoon the mixture over the chicken.

Goes well with

  • Crock Pot Chicken, Cheese & Potatoes
  • Baked Pork Chops
