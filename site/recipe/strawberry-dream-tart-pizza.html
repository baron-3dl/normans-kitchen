Strawberry Dream Tart (Pizza)

A no-bake strawberry tart with a cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla pudding filling spread over a corn flake brown sugar crust and topped with fresh sliced strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 1 8 oz.pkg cream cheese, softened
  • 1 14 oz.can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 5.1 ozpkg vanilla instant pudding
  • 1/2 cupwater
  • 1 8 ozcontainer frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Brown Sugar Crust
  • 1 qtfresh strawberries, sliced
  • SUGAR CRUST
  • 4 cupscorn flakes cereal
  • 3/4 cupbutter or margarine, softened
  • 1/4 cupfirmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tspalmond extract
  • 1 1/3 cupsall purpose flour

Directions

  1. Beat cream cheese at medium speed with an electric mixer until smooth. Add next 3 ingredients; beat until smooth. Chill 5 minutes; fold in whipped topping.
  2. Spread filling over Brown Sugar Crust; arrange strawberries over filling. Chill at lest 30 minutes before slicing. Yield: one 12-inch tart.

Gradually add flour and cereal. Shape mixture into a ball; press into a well buttered 12-inch pizza pan. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely.   2. Beat the butter and brown sugar at medium speed with an electric mixer until fluffy; add the egg yolks, salt, and almond extract, beating until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the flour and the crushed cereal.
  4. Shape the mixture into a ball, then press into a well-buttered 12-inch pizza pan.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool completely. (Yield: one 12-inch crust.)
  6. Make the filling: Beat the cream cheese at medium speed with an electric mixer until smooth. Add the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla instant pudding, and water; beat until smooth.
  7. Chill 5 minutes, then fold in the whipped topping.
  8. Spread the filling over the cooled Brown Sugar Crust; arrange the sliced strawberries over the filling.
  9. Chill at least 30 minutes before slicing. (Yield: one 12-inch tart.)
