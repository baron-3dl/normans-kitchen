Crunchy Lace Cookies

Thin, crisp drop cookies made from oats, coconut, and brown sugar, bound with melted butter and corn syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsphot water
  • 1 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • 3/4 cupbutter or margarine melted
  • 1 1/2 cupsuncooked regular oats
  • 1 tbsplight corn syrup
  • 1 1/2 cupspacked light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cupsall purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cupsflaked coconut

Directions

  1. STIR together 2 tablespoons hot water and baking soda in a large bowl until baking soda is dissolved. Add butter and syrup, stirring until blended.
  2. COMBINE 1 1/2 cups oats and next 3 ingredients; stir into butter mixture, stirring until blended.
  3. SHAPE dough into 3/4 inch balls, and place 3 inches apart, onto lightly greased baking sheets. Slightly flatten dough balls.
  4. BAKE at 350 degrees for 7 to 8 minutes or until golden brown. Remove to wire rack to cool.

  2. Add the melted butter and corn syrup, stirring until blended.
  3. Combine the 1 1/2 cups oats with the brown sugar, flour, and coconut; stir into the butter mixture until blended.
  4. Shape the dough into 3/4-inch balls and place 3 inches apart on lightly greased baking sheets. Slightly flatten the dough balls.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 7 to 8 minutes, until golden brown. Remove to a wire rack to cool.

Goes well with

  • Big Brownie Oatmeal Drops
  • Peanut Butter and Honey Oatmeal Cookies
  • Macaroon Cookies
