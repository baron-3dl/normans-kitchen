Snowball Fudge

A stovetop chocolate fudge studded with tiny marshmallows and topped with chopped nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cupssugar
  • 1 smcan evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 pkgtiny marshmallows
  • 1 cupsemi sweet chocolate bits
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1/2 cupchopped nuts

Directions

  1. Blend sugar, evaporated milk and salt in a saucepan, heat to boiling, boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly, remove from heat.
  2. Stir in half the marshmallows (about 1 1/2 cups) chocolate chips and vanilla, stir until melted and blended. Pour into buttered square pan, sprinkle nuts on top, swirl lightly into candy. Chill, then cut into squares.

Goes well with

  • Heavenly Hash
