Slow Cooked Pepper Steak

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbsbeef round steak
  • 2 tbspcooking oil
  • 1/4 cupsoy sauce
  • 1 cupchopped onions
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 1 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1/4 tspground ginger
  • 4tomatoes cut in 8ths (can use canned tomatoes)
  • 2 lgegreen pepper, cut into strips
  • 1.2 cupscold water
  • 1 tbspcornstarch
  • noodle, rice, chow mein

Directions

  1. Cut beef into 3 inch by 1 inch strips. Brown in oil in a skillet. Transfer to a slow cooker. Combine the next seven ingredients; pour over beef. Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours or until meat is tender. Add tomatoes and green peppers; cook on low for 1 hour longer. Combine the cold water and cornstarch to make a paste; stir into liquid in slow cooker and cook on high until thickened. Serve over noodles or rice.

Savory Pepper Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbsround steak 1/2-in thick
  • 1/4 cupflour
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1 medonion, chopped
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 2 lgegreen peppers, cut 1/12 in strips
  • 1 cantomatoes
  • 1 tbsbeef flavor granules
  • 1 tbssoy sauce
  • 2 tspWorcestershire sauce

Directions

  1. Cut steak into strips. Combine flour; salt and pepper; toss with steak strips to coat thoroughly. Add to stoneware with onion, garlic and half of pepper strips; stir. Combine tomatoes with beef base, soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Pour into stoneware, moistening meat well. Cover, cook on low 10 to 12 hours (High 5 to 6 hrs) One hour before serving, stir in remaining pepper strips. Serve over hot rice.
