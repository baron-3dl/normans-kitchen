Sweet Chocolate Pie

Photo: stu_spivack (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A frozen chocolate pie of melted sweet baking chocolate blended with cream cheese and whipped topping in a graham cracker crust.

Ingredients

  • 4 ozpkg sweet baking chocolate
  • 1/3 cupmilk
  • 4 ozcream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbssugar
  • 12 ozfrozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1graham cracker crust

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate and 2 tablespoons of the milk in pan until melted.
  2. Beat in cream cheese, sugar and remaining milk with wire whisk until blended. Refrigerate about 10 minutes to cool. Gently stir in 3 1/2 cups of the whipped topping until smooth. Spoon into crust. Freeze 4 hours. Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes before cutting pie. Serve with remaining whipped topping. Store leftovers in freezer.
