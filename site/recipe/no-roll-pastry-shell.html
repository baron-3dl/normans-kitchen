No Roll Pastry Shell

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupssifted all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tspsugar
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/2 cupoil
  • 2 tbscold milk

Directions

  1. sift dry ingredients into pie pan. Combine oil and milk in measuring cup. Whip with fork and pour all at once over flour mixture. Mix with fork until flour is completely dampened. Press evenly and firmly over bottom and sides of pan.
