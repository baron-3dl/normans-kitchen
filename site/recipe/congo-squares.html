Congo Squares

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cupssifted flour
  • 2 1/2 tspbaking powder
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 2/3 cupshortening
  • 2 1/4 cupsbrown sugar
  • 3eggs
  • 1 cupnuts
  • 1 pkgchocolate chips

Directions

  1. Sift flour, baking powder and salt. Melt shortening, add sugar. Cool slightly, add eggs, one at a time, beat well. Add dry ingredients, nuts and chocolate. Bake a 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
