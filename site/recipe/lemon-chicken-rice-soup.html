Lemon Chicken Rice Soup

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 2 cupsuncooked rice
  • 1/2 cuplemon juice
  • 1/2 gallonhomemade chicken stock
  • 2 cupsdiced carrots
  • 2 cupsdiced celery
  • 1 cupdiced onions
  • diced white meat from the whole chicken used to make stock
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tspchopped parsley

Directions

  1. Cook rice until tender. When cool add lemon juice.
  2. Cook vegetables in chicken stock until tender. Add rice mixture, diced chicken and parsley, Season to taste with salt and pepper.
