Baked Chicken and Rice w/Mushrooms

A one-pan baked casserole of chicken pieces layered over long-grain and wild rice with fresh mushrooms in a cream of chicken soup sauce.

Ingredients

  • 6 ozpkg. Uncle Ben's long-grain or wild rice
  • 3 lbsskinless thighs or legs or breasts
  • 1/2 envonion soup mix
  • 8 ozsliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 can10 1/4 oz. cream of chicken soup
  • 2 1/4 cupswater

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Spread rice in roasting pan; reserve seasoning packet. Top rice with chicken. Sprinkle with dry soup mix and mushrooms. Spoon condensed soup over rice and chicken. Sprinkle herbs from rice mix over all. Pour water over ingredients. Cover.
  3. Bake 2-2 1/2 hours. Don't open or stir until cooking is over.

Goes well with
