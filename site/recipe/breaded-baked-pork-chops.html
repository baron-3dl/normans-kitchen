Breaded Baked Pork Chops

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 leanpork chops 1/2 in thick
  • 1egg white
  • 1 cupevaporated milk
  • 3/4 cupscornflake crumbs
  • 1/4 cupfine bread crumbs
  • 4 tsppaprika
  • 2 tsporegano
  • 3/4 tspchili powder
  • 1/2 tspgarlic powder
  • 1/2 tspblack pepper
  • 1/8 tspcayenne pepper
  • 1/8 tspdry mustard
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Trim all feta from chops. Beat egg white with evaporated milk. Place cops in milk mixture and let stand for 5 minutes, tuning chops once.
  2. Meanwhile, mix together cornflake crumbs, spices, and salt. Spray a 9 x 13 in baking pan with nonstick spray. Remove chops from milk mixture. Coat thoroughly with crumb mixture,
  3. Place chops in baking dish and bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes. Turn chops and bake 15 minutes longer or until no longer pink. Makes 6 servings.
