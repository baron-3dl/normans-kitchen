Brownies

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Cream 1/2 cup butter (1 stick), add 1 cup sugar. Mix in 2 eggs (eaten lightly). Add 1 tsp vanilla. Stir in 2/3 cup sifted flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, add 2 squares bitter chocolate (melted) and 2/3 cup chopped nuts
  2. Use 8 in square pan, lined with wax paper. Bake at 325 for 35 minutes.
