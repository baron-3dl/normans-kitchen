Chocolate Cheese Squares Sex in a Pan

Chocolate Cheese Squares

Photo: kurmanstaff (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • 1 1/2 sticksmargarine, melted
  • 1 3/4 cupsflour
  • 1/2 cupchopped nuts
  • Filling
  • 1 pkg8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pkg3 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cupconfectioners sugar
  • 1/2 lgecontainer of cool whip spread
  • 2 pkginstant chocolate pudding mix
  • 3 cupsmilk

Directions

  1. Crust: blend margarine flour and nuts altogether. Roll out and place in pan and up the sides of the pan. (9 x 13 x 2 pan)
  2. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes. Set aside to Cool
  3. Blend both packages of cheese and confectioners sugar together. Mix in cool whip. Then fill crust with mixture.
  4. Blend 2 packages instant chocolate pudding, 3 cups milk, beat well. Pour over cream cheese mixture and let it set for a few hours.
  5. Spread remaining cool whip on top of pudding mixture.
  6. When cool and set cut into bars and enjoy.

Sex in a Pan

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 stickmargarine
  • 1/2 cupnuts or heath bars (crushed)
  • Topping
  • 8 ozcream cheese
  • 1/3lge container cool whipped cream
  • 1/2 cuppowdered sugar
  • Filling
  • 2 pkgchocolate instant pudding
  • 3 cupscold milk

Directions

  1. Mix flour, margarine and nuts or heath bits together to form crust. Pat into a 13 x 9 pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes. Stays pale. Cool
  2. Mix pudding with milk and beat until thickens and spread over crust.
  3. Mix cream cheese, cool whip and powdered sugar until smooth. Spread on top of pudding. Set in refrigerator until needed.
