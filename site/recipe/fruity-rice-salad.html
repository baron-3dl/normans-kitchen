Fruity Rice salad

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg3 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cupplain yogurt
  • 1/4 cuphoney
  • 2 tbslemon juice
  • 1 tspgrated lemon peel
  • 2 cupslong grain cooked rice, cooled
  • 3 freshpeaches, chopped or 1 1/2 cup frozen
  • 1 cupgreen grapes, halved
  • 1/2 cupsliced celery
  • 1/2 cupraisins
  • 1/2 cupchopped pecans

Directions

  1. IN a mixing bowl, combine the first five ingredients; beat until smooth. Add the rice, peaches, grapes, celery and raisins; stir well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours. Just before serving, stir into he pecans.
