Praline Cookies

Photo: thebittenword.com (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cupplus 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 cupsbrown sugar
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 cupplus 2 tbsp AP flour
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1 1/4 cupspecan halves

Directions

  1. Combine first 4 ingredients stirring well. add flour and salt, stir well. Stir in pecans. Drop by tbsp on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes
