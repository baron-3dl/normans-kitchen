Potatoes Anna

Photo: stu_spivack (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 6 lgepotatoes sliced thin
  • 1/2 cupmelted butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika

Directions

  1. Dips each slice potato in butter and layer in circles in greased 9 inch iron skillet. Season each layer with spices. Cover ad bake 450 degrees 15 to 20 minutes. Loosen, invert onto platter
