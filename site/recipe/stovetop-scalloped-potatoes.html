Stovetop Scalloped Potatoes

Scalloped potatoes made entirely on the stovetop, layering sliced potatoes with Gruyere, onions, flour, and milk in a skillet until tender and thickened.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsbutter
  • 1 1/3 lbspotatoes, sliced 1/8 in hick
  • 1 cupshredded Gruyere
  • 1/2 cupthinly sliced onions
  • 1 tbsflour
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 cuplow fat milk

Directions

  1. Spread half of the butter on the bottom of a 12 inch nonstick skillet. Layer half of the potato slices, half ot the cheese and then half of the onions.
  2. Sprinkle with half of the flour. Dot with half of the remaining butter. Sprinkle with half of the salt and half of he pepper. Repeat layers ending with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour milk over all. Bring to simmering over high heat. Reduce heat to medium low, cover and cook until potato slices are tender, about 20 minutes.
  4. Uncover skillet and simmer until sauce thickens, about 7 minutes.
