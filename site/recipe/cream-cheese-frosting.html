Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg3 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tbsmilk
  • 2 1/2 cupssifted confectioners sugar
  • 1/2 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Cream all ingredients together to blend into smooth frosting.
  2. Melted chocolate can be added to make a chocolate frosting.
