Appleless Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 cupshot water
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 2 tspcream of tartar
  • 22Ritz crackers

Directions

  1. Mix water, sugar and cream of tartar. Drop in one at a time 22 Ritz crackers, bring to a boil for 2 minutes, (do not stir), let cool.
  2. Make a crust and pour in filling, sprinkle with cinnamon and small pieces of utter, put top crust on and bake in 450 degree oven for 15 minutes and then lower oven to 350 and bake until brown.
