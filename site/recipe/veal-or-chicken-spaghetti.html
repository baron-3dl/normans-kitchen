Veal (or Chicken) Spaghetti

Serves 4 but may be increased to

Photo: Ceeseven (CC BY-SA 4.0)

Ingredients

  • 2 medgreen peppers cut in strips
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 2 lbsveal cutlets cut in 1" pieces
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 14 oz.can tomatoes
  • 1/4 lbmushrooms
  • 1 smallclove garlic
  • 1/4 cupdry red wine
  • 2 cupscooked spaghetti
  • oregano
  • basil to taste

Directions

  1. Saute in oil, the green pepper till tender, remove from pan. Season veal (or chicken) with salt/pepper, cook over medium heat until lightly brown. Add green pepper and remaining ingredients. Simmer uncovered one hour til meat is tender. Serve over spaghetti. Add more tomatoes or water to increase sauce. I enjoy adding onions also. Wine can be eliminated. Serves 4 but may be increased to serve more.
