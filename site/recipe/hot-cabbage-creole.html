Hot Cabbage Creole

A skillet of chopped cabbage stewed with bacon, onion, green pepper, and tomatoes, seasoned with vinegar and red pepper for a Creole-style side.

Ingredients

  • 2 slicesbacon, chopped in small pieces
  • 1 largeonion chopped
  • 1 largegreen pepper chopped
  • 1 can28 oz tomatoes chopped
  • 1 medcabbage chopped
  • 1/3 cupvinegar
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper plus dash red peppers

Directions

  1. Fry bacon in Dutch oven. Add onion and green pepper, saute till tender. Add remaining ingredients, cover, bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer about 15 minutes.
  1. Fry the chopped bacon in a Dutch oven.
  2. Add the chopped onion and green pepper; sauté until tender.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients (the chopped tomatoes, chopped cabbage, vinegar, salt, pepper, and dash of red pepper). Cover and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer about 15 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Cole Slaw
  • Easy Cabbage and Beef Rolls
  • Chourico Stewed w/Cabbage
  • Creole Sauce
