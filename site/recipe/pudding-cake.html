Pudding Cake

Photo: joyosity (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgjiffy yellow cake mix
  • 1 pkgvanilla or coconut pudding
  • 1 cancrushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 pkg8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cupwalnuts
  • 1 lgecool whip
  • cherries

Directions

  1. Mix cake mix and pour into 13 x 9 inch pan. Bake 20-25 minutes. Cool. Mix pudding using a little less milk than called for. Blend pudding and cream cheese until smooth, spread over cooled cake, spread pineapple over all, spread cool whip over that, spread walnuts over cream dot with cherries.
