Sorrento Chicken Squisito

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsolive oil
  • 4skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder
  • 1 medonion, diced
  • 1 can14.5 oz Italian seasoned diced tomatoes
  • 1.2 cupschicken broth
  • 1 cupMozzarella, shredded
  • 1/3 cupgrated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cupgrated Romano

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Season chicken with powders, salt and pepper. Heat over medium high heat in an oven safe pan. When pan is hot, add chicken and saute 2 minutes on each side. Remove chicken. Saute onions in pan for 4 minutes. Add tomatoes and broth and cook for 8 minutes to blend flavors. Place reserved chicken in pan, stir to combine, Mix cheeses together and sprinkle over chicken. Bake about 15 minutes. Serve with ice or pasta.
