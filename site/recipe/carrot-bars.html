Carrot Bars

Ingredients

  • 4eggs
  • 2 cupssugar
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 1/2 cupssalad oil
  • 2 tspbaking soda
  • 2 tspcinnamon
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 3 cupsfinely grated carrots
  • 1 1/2 cupsflaked coconut
  • 1 1/2 cupswalnuts

Directions

  1. Beat eggs until light, gradually beat in sugar. Alternately add salad oil and flour sifted with baking soda and cinnamon and salt. Mix well, fold in carrots, nuts, coconut. Spread evenly in 2 greased 12 x 9 x 2 inch pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
