Carrot Bars

Photo: Vieve Forward (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Spiced carrot cake baked as bars, made with grated carrots, walnuts, and flaked coconut in two large pans.

Ingredients

  • 4eggs
  • 2 cupssugar
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 1/2 cupssalad oil
  • 2 tspbaking soda
  • 2 tspcinnamon
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 3 cupsfinely grated carrots
  • 1 1/2 cupsflaked coconut
  • 1 1/2 cupswalnuts

Directions

  1. Beat eggs until light, gradually beat in sugar. Alternately add salad oil and flour sifted with baking soda and cinnamon and salt. Mix well, fold in carrots, nuts, coconut. Spread evenly in 2 greased 12 x 9 x 2 inch pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
  1. Beat the eggs until light, then gradually beat in the sugar.
  2. Alternately add the salad oil and the flour sifted with the baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Fold in the carrots, walnuts, and coconut.
  4. Spread evenly in 2 greased 12 x 9 x 2-inch pans.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.

Goes well with

  • Carrot Cake
  • Hidden Frosting Carrot Zucchini Cake
  • Cream Cheese Frosting
