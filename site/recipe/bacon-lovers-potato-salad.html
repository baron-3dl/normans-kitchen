Bacon Lover's Potato Salad

Photo: thienzieyung (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 5 medpotatoes cut in half
  • 4 slicesbacon
  • 1/2 cupfinely diced onions
  • 1 cupdiced celery
  • 3 tbsmayonnaise
  • 1/3 cupfat free sour cream
  • 1 tspsugar
  • 1/2 tspseasoned salt
  • Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes in heir skins in a covered saucepan until tender but not mushy (about 25 minutes). When they are cool enough to handle, remove skins and dice the potatoes into a large serving bowl.
  2. Fry the bacon over low heat flipping often in a nonstick skillet until crisp. Drain well on paper towels and break into coarse bits of bacon; set aside.
  3. Add the onion and celery to the serving bowl with the potatoes.
  4. In the work bowl of a food processor fitted with a metal blade, combine bacon bits, mayonnaise, sour cream,sugar, seasoned salt and pepper. Pulse several times to blend. If the machine dies not have a pulse button, press the on-off button several times. Stir gently into potato mixture until well combined.
  5. Chill the potato salad until you are ready to serve.
