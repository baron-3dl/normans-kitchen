Bittersweet Topping for choc. cake

A light whipped topping for chocolate cake, made by folding melted semisweet chocolate loosely into stiffly whipped cream for a mottled effect.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Melt 3 oz semisweet chocolate bit, cool slightly. Whip 3/4 cup heavy cream stiff. Add cooled chocolate, fold into cream very lightly for mottled effect.
  1. Melt the 3 oz semisweet chocolate bits and cool slightly.
  2. Whip the 3/4 cup heavy cream until stiff.
  3. Add the cooled chocolate and fold it into the cream very lightly for a mottled effect.

Goes well with

  • Dark Chocolate Sauce
  • Perfectly Chocolate Cake
  • Light Chocolate Cake
