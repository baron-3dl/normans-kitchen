Mattapoisett Inn French Herb Dressing

A tangy French-style herb dressing built on olive oil and tarragon vinegar, brightened with scallions, parsley, garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 bchscallions, chopped fine
  • 1 bchparsley, chopped fine
  • 1/2 cuplemon juice
  • 4 clovesgarlic chopped
  • 1/2 cupDijon mustard
  • 1/2 cupchili sauce
  • 1/2 cupmayonnaise
  • 2 cupsolive oil
  • 2 cupstarragon vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Whisk together the olive oil and tarragon vinegar and add the remaining ingredients.
  1. In a bowl, whisk together the 2 cups olive oil and 2 cups tarragon vinegar.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients: the finely chopped scallions, finely chopped parsley, 1/2 cup lemon juice, 4 chopped garlic cloves, 1/2 cup Dijon mustard, 1/2 cup chili sauce, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, and salt and pepper to taste. Whisk until combined.

Goes well with

  • R. C. Shea's House Dressing
  • 1-Minute French Dressing
  • Tomato Dijon Dressing
