Soupe au Pistou

Photo: Jdclevenger at English Wikipedia (original text: photo by Jason Clevenger) (CC BY-SA 2.5)

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsolive oil
  • 1 medonion, chopped
  • 1 medcarrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 stalkcelery, chopped
  • 1 smleek white part chopped
  • 7 xwater
  • 1 cupchicken broth
  • 2 medtomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tspherbs de Provence or dried thyme and oregano leaves
  • 2 tspsalt
  • 12 tspblack pepper
  • 1 medpotato, peeled, diced tomatoes
  • 1 cupfresh green beans cut in 1 in pieces
  • 1 can15 oz. Cannelloni beans
  • PISTOU
  • 2 cupsfresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cupolive oil
  • 2 clovesgarlic, pressed or finely chopped

Directions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, celery and leek. Reduce heat to low and saute for 10 minutes. Add water, broth, tomatoes, herbs, salt and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes. Add potato and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in green beans and cannelloni beans and cook until all vegetables are tender, about 8 minutes. Serve in bowls with a spoonful of Pistou.
  2. FOR PISTOU: In a food processor, combine all ingredients. Process until pureed. If not using immediately, cover and refrigerate up to 5 days.
