Whoopie Pies

Ingredients

  • 6 tbspCrisco
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1egg
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 cupmilk
  • 2 cupsflour
  • 1 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • 5 tbspCocoa
  • Filling
  • 1/2 cupmargarine
  • 1/2 cupbutter
  • 1 cupconfectioner's sugar
  • 1 cupmarshmallow fluff
  • 2 tspvanilla

Directions

  1. Cream together the first 6 ingredients. Blend the dry ingredients together and combine both. Drop by small tablespoonful on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 425 degrees for about 7 minutes.
  2. Remove to rack to cool. When cool, match tops and bottoms to size and fill with filling.
