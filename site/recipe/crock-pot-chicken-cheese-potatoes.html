Crock Pot Chicken, Cheese & Potatoes

A slow-cooker casserole of chicken breasts layered with thinly sliced potatoes in a sauce of cream of cheddar cheese soup, cream of chicken soup, and milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbschicken breasts
  • 3 medpotatoes
  • 1 cancream cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 cancream chicken soup
  • 1 cupskim milk
  • 2 bsgarlic powder (optional)
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mix soups and milk together. Slice potatoes thinly. Put 1/4 mixture in bottom of crock pot and layer potatoes. Put another 1/4 mixture on top of potatoes. Sprinkle chicken with garlic powder and place on top. Put remaining soup on top of chicken.
  2. Cook 6-7 hours on low. OR High 3-4 hours.
  1. Mix the two soups (cream of cheddar cheese and cream of chicken) together with the skim milk.
  2. Slice the potatoes thinly.
  3. Put 1/4 of the soup mixture in the bottom of the crock pot, then layer the potatoes on top.
  4. Put another 1/4 of the soup mixture on top of the potatoes.
  5. Sprinkle the chicken with garlic powder and place on top.
  6. Put the remaining soup mixture on top of the chicken.
  7. Cover and cook 6-7 hours on low, or 3-4 hours on high.

Goes well with

  • Crock Pot Chicken or Pork Chops
  • Cheesy Potato Casserole
  • Three Cheese Chicken Bake
