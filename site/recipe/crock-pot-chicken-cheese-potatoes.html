Crock Pot Chicken, Cheese & Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbschicken breasts
  • 3 medpotatoes
  • 1 cancream cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 cancream chicken soup
  • 1 cupskim milk
  • 2 bsgarlic powder (optional)
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mix soups and milk together. Slice potatoes thinly. Put 1/4 mixture in bottom of crock pot and layer potatoes. Put another 1/4 mixture on top of potatoes. Sprinkle chicken with garlic powder and place on top. Put remaining soup on top of chicken.
  2. Cook 6-7 hours on low. OR High 3-4 hours.
