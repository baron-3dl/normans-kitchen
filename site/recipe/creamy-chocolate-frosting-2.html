Creamy Chocolate Frosting

Photo: ginnerobot (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A chocolate frosting made from melted butter, cocoa, salt, and vanilla with confectioners sugar and milk beaten in until smooth and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsmelted butter
  • 1/4 cupcocoa
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 3 tbsmilk
  • 1 3/4 cupsconfectioners sugar

Directions

  1. Combine butter, cocoa, salt and vanilla. Add sugar and milk alternately, mixing until smooth and creamy.
  1. Combine the melted butter, cocoa, salt, and vanilla.
  2. Add the confectioners sugar and milk alternately, mixing until smooth and creamy.

Goes well with

  • Perfectly Chocolate Cake
  • Chocolate Sour Cream Cake
  • Wacky Cake

Creamy Chocolate Frosting · 2

Photo: ginnerobot (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A quick chocolate frosting made by stirring together powdered sugar, cocoa, salt, skim milk, and vanilla until spreadable.

Ingredients

  • 3 cupssifted powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cupunsweetened cocoa
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 cupskim milk
  • 1 1/2 tspvanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl, stirring until frosting is of spreading consistency.
  1. Combine all ingredients (powdered sugar, cocoa, salt, skim milk, and vanilla) in a medium bowl.
  2. Stir until the frosting is of spreading consistency.

Goes well with

  • Perfectly Chocolate Cake
  • Light Chocolate Cake
  • Wacky Cake
