Portuguese Pork Chops

Pork chops fried and simmered with garlic, onion, mushrooms, and green peppers in a tomato-and-wine sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8chops
  • 1 clovegarlic
  • 1onion, minced
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 tbstomato puree
  • 2 cupschopped mushrooms
  • 2green peppers minced
  • 1/4 cupwine

Directions

  1. Fry veggies and chops the cover and simmer for at least 1/2 hour.
  1. Fry veggies and chops the cover and simmer for at least 1/2 hour.

Goes well with

  • Cacoila
  • Nana's Portuguese Roast Chicken & Potatoes
  • Pork Chop Ole'
