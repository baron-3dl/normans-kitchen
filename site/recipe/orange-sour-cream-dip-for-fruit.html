Orange Sour Cream Dip for Fruit

A creamy fruit dip made from frozen orange juice, milk, vanilla instant pudding, and sour cream, served in pineapple boats with pineapple, grapefruit, oranges, and grapes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup6 oz frozen orange juice
  • 1 1/4 cupscold milk
  • 1 pkgvanilla instant pudding
  • 1/4 cupsour cream
  • 1 medpineapple or 1 can pineapple chunks
  • 1grapefruit
  • 3oranges
  • 2 lbsgrapes

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl combine orange juice and milk. With electric mixer beat in pudding on low speed 2 minutes. Stir in sour cream.
  2. Cut pineapple in half lengthwise, remove fruit. Cut all fruit into bite size pieces.
  3. Put dip into pineapple boats and use wooden picks for the fruit.
