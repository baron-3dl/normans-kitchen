Frozen Mocha Cheesecake Loaf

Serves 12

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupsfat free chocolate sandwich crumbs
  • 4 1/2 tspbutter, melted
  • 1 pkg8 oz fat free cream cheese
  • 1 can14 oz fat fat free condensed milk
  • 1 tbsvanilla extract
  • 4 cupsreduced far whipped topping
  • 7 tspinstant coffee granules
  • 1 tbshot water
  • 1/2 cupchocolate syrup

Directions

  1. Line a 9 inch by 5 by 3 inch loaf pan with heavy duty foil. In a small bowl, combine cookie crumbs and butter. Press firmly onto bottom and 1/2 inch up sides of prepared pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until light and fluffy. Add milk and vanilla; mix well. Fold in whipped topping. Spoon half of the mixture into another bowl and set aside.
  3. Dissolve coffee granules in hot water; fold into remaining cream cheese mixture. Fold in chocolate syrup.
  4. Spoon half other chocolate mixture over crust. top with half of the reserved cream cheese mixture. repeat layers (pan will be full). Freeze, uncovered, for 6 hours or until set. Cover and freeze until serving. Use foil to lift out of pan; cut into slices. Serves 12
