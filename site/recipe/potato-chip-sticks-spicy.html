Potato Chip Sticks Spicy

Strips of boneless chicken breast dipped in a spicy garlic-oil mixture, coated in crushed potato chips, and baked until crisp; served with a three-mustard dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbsskinless/boneless breast cut into strips
  • 3 clovesgarlic minced
  • 1 cuplight olive oil
  • few drops Tabasco
  • 1 1/2 cupscrushed potato chips
  • MUSTARD SAUCE
  • 1/2 cupGerman Mustard
  • 1/2 cupDijon mustard
  • 1 tbsChinese mustard
  • 1/3light cream
  • 1/2 cupMiracle Whip

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees. Place garlic, oil and hot pepper sauce in food processor or blender, process until smooth and pour into bowl. Place crushed potato chips in another bowl. Dip chicken in garlic mixture, then in chips. Place in a single layer on a shallow baking pan. Drizzle remaining garlic mixture over chicken and sprinkle on remaining chips. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until brown and crispy. Serve with Mustard sauce.
  2. Mixed Mustard Sauce: Combine mustards, honey and cream in small bowl. Whisk in Miracle Whip Salad Dressing until smooth.
