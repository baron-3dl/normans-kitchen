1-2-3 Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 lbsred or new potatoes, cut into 3/4 inch chunks
  • 1Knorr Chicken or Vegetable Bouillon Cube
  • 1 cupMayonnaise

Directions

  1. In 4 quart saucepot, cover potatoes and bouillon cube with water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat and cook uncovered 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Drain. Do not rinse. Let cool slightly.
  2. In large bowl, combine cooled potatoes with mayonnaise. Chill, if desired, before serving. Garnish with parsley and black pepper.
