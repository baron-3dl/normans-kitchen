Bread Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupbutter or margarine, softened
  • 1 pkg4 serving instant vanilla pudding
  • 1 tspground cinnamon
  • 3eggs
  • 3 cupsmilk
  • 1 can8 oz crushed pineapples with juice
  • 2/3 cupflake coconut milk
  • 1/2 cupraisins
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 8 slicesday old bread cut into 1/2 inch cubes

Directions

  1. In large bowl cream together margarine, vanilla pudding and cinnamon til fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beat well after each. In another bowl combine milk, undrained pineapples, coconut, raisins and vanilla. By hand, blend mixture into creamed mixture, it will look curdled. Fold in bread cubes. Pour into ungreased 8 x 8 x 2 baking pan. Place baking dish into larger pan on oven rack. Pour hot water into larger pan to a depth of 1 inch. Bake at 325 degrees for about 1 hour to 1 1/4 hours till knife inserted in center comes out clean.
