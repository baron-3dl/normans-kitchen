Swedish Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 canapple pie filling
  • 1 tbssugar
  • 1 tspcinnamon
  • 3/4 cupbutter or margarine
  • 1 cupsugar
  • 1 cupflour
  • 1egg unbeaten
  • pinchsalt
  • 1/2 cupraisins
  • 1/2 cupchopped nuts

Directions

  1. Butter well a 9 inch pie plate. Fill with apple filling, sprinkle with tsp sugar and cinnamon, set aside
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter, add sugar, flour, 1 egg unbeaten, pinch of salt, raisins or nuts. Mix well pour over apples. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serve with ice cream or
