Savory Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cupMiracle Whip Salad Dressing
  • 1/4 cuplemon juice
  • 2 tbsporegano leaves
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 2-3 lbsbroiler chicken cut up

Directions

  1. Combine salad dressing, juice and seasonings, mix well. Pour over chicken; chill several hours or overnight. Reserve salad dressing mixture; place chicken, skin side up on rack in baking pan. Bake at 425 degrees for 40 minutes, brushing occasionally with extra dressing.
