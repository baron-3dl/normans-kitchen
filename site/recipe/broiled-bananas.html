Broiled Bananas

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupbrown sugar
  • 1 tbsplime juice
  • 4bananas sliced

Directions

  1. Mix sugar and juice. Coat sliced bananas. Place in ungreased dish and broil until sugar has turned to a glaze. 3-5 minutes. Serve with ice cream or sour cream.
