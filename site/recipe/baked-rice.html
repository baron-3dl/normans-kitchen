Oven-Baked Rice

Poppa kept 2 versions of this — pick one:

Baked Rice Oven Rice

Baked Rice

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupchopped onions
  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • 1 cuprice
  • 2 cupschicken broth
  • 1/4 cupchopped parsley
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Saute onion in butter til translucent. Add rice and stir till coated. Bring chicken stock to boil, add parsley and salt. Pour into 1 quart casserole, cover and bake at 375 degrees about 20 minutes or liquid is gone. Serves 6

Oven Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupsuncooked regular rice
  • 2 1/2 cupschicken broth
  • 1 1/2 tbspcelery chopped fine
  • 1 1/2 tbsponion chopped fine
  • 1 1/2 tsppepper
  • 1 1/2 tbspmargarine
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/8 tspgarlic powder
  • dashred pepper
  • pinchwhite pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in 2 quart baking dish, stir well. Cover and bake at 350 degrees 1 hour or until liquid is all gone.
