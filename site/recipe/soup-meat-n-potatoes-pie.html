Soup Meat 'n Potatoes Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cancream of mushroom soup
  • 1 lbground beef
  • 1/4 cupfinely chopped onion
  • 1egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 cupfine dry bread crumbs
  • 2 tbschopped parsley
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • dashpepper
  • 2 cupsmashed potatoes
  • 1/4 cupshredded cheese

Directions

  1. Mix thoroughly 1/2 cup soup, beef, onion, egg, bread crumbs, parsley, and seasonings. Press firmly into 9 inch pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes; spoon off fat. Frost with potatoes; op with remaining soup and cheese. Bake 10 minutes more or until done. Garnish with cooked bacon if desired. Makes on 9-inch meat pie.
