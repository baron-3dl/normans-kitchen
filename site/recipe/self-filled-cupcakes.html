Self-Filled Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 pkgDevils Food Cake Mix
  • 1 8 oz.cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/3 cupsugar
  • 1egg
  • dashsalt
  • 1 6 oz.semi sweet choc chips

Directions

  1. Prepare cake mix according to package. Spoon batter into muffin tins with paper cups. Combine cream cheese and sugar, cream until light and fluffy. Add egg and salt beat well. Stir in chocolate chips. Spoon 1 tsp cream cheese mixture into center of each cupcake. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Cool in pan 10 minutes remove to wire rack.
