Orange Chocolate Cheesecake

Photo: Tobyotter (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1 squnsweetened chocolate
  • 2 pkg8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 6eggs, separated
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 1 tbsgrated orange rind
  • 1 tbsorange juice
  • 1 cupwhipping cream
  • Crust
  • 2 cupsgraham crackers
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/4 tspcinnamon
  • 1/8 tspnutmeg
  • 1/2 cupbutter

Directions

  1. CRUST: Combine crumbs, sugar and spices, cut in butter until completly blended, Press mixture on bottom and sides of pan. Chill.
  2. CHEESECAKE: Melt chocolate. Meanwhile blend cream cheese and sugar until creamy and light. Add egg yolks one at a time, beating well after each addition, stir in vanilla,. orange peel and juice. Beat egg whites until stiff, spoon onto cheese mixture. Whip cream,fold with egg whites into cheese mixture until well blended. Spoon 1/3 of filling into crumb lined pan, trickle a little melted chocolate over surface swirl lightly. Repeat twice. Bake in very slow oven 300 degrees 1 hour. turn heat off, leave cake in oven with door closed 1 hour longer. Remove from oven, cool at room temperature, then chill.
