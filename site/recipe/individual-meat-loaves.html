Individual Meat Loaves

Individual meat loaves made from lean ground beef and ground pork sausage bound with bread cubes, egg, and milk, then topped with chili sauce and baked.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbslean ground beef
  • 1/2 lbground pork sausage
  • 1 smallonion, chopped
  • 1 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1/2 tsppoultry seasoning
  • 2 tbspWorcestershire sauce
  • 5 sliceswhite breadcrumbs
  • 2 lgeeggs
  • 1/2 cupmilk
  • 1 1/2 cupssoft breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/2 cupschili sauce
  • 1/2 cupboiling water

Directions

  1. Combine first 7 ingredients; set aside. Remove crusts from bread; cut bread into cubes. Combine bread cubes, eggs and milk in mixing bowl; beat at medium speed with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir in meat mixture, and shape into 6 individual loaves; roll each in bread crumbs. Place in a lightly greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. Spread chili sauce over loaves. Pour boiling water into dish. Bake, uncovered a 350 degrees for 1 hour. Transfer loaves to a serving platter; garnish if desired. Yield; 6 servings.
  1. Combine the first 7 ingredients (ground beef, ground pork sausage, onion, salt, pepper, poultry seasoning, and Worcestershire sauce); set aside.
  2. Remove the crusts from the 5 slices of white bread; cut the bread into cubes.
  3. Combine the bread cubes, eggs, and milk in a mixing bowl; beat at medium speed with an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Stir in the meat mixture, and shape into 6 individual loaves; roll each in the soft breadcrumbs.
  5. Place the loaves in a lightly greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. Spread the chili sauce over the loaves.
  6. Pour the boiling water into the pan. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  7. Transfer the loaves to a serving platter; garnish if desired. Yield: 6 servings.
