Chourico Stewed w/Cabbage

A Portuguese-style stew of sliced chourico sausage simmered with finely chopped cabbage, onion, and garlic in soup stock.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbchourico, chopped up
  • 2 qtssoup stock
  • 1 smfinely chopped cabbage
  • 2 tbsolive oil
  • 2chopped onions
  • 1 clovegarlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Saute onion and garlic in oil. Slice chourico and add with remaining ingredients. Simmer until cabbage is well cooked.
  1. Saute the onions and garlic in the olive oil.
  2. Slice the chourico and add it along with the remaining ingredients: soup stock, chopped cabbage, and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Simmer until the cabbage is well cooked.

Goes well with

  • Cacoila
  • Bev's Recipe
  • Sausage and Pepper Ragout
  • Hot Dogs/ Sweet Sour Cabbage
