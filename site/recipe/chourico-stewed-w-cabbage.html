Chourico Stewed w/Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 lbchourico, chopped up
  • 2 qtssoup stock
  • 1 smfinely chopped cabbage
  • 2 tbsolive oil
  • 2chopped onions
  • 1 clovegarlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Saute onion and garlic in oil. Slice chourico and add with remaining ingredients. Simmer until cabbage is well cooked.
