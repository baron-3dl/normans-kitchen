Broccoli Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 stickbutter
  • 1onion, chopped
  • 2 pkgfrozen cut up broccoli
  • 1 cancream mushroom soup
  • 1 cupmilk
  • 1 3/4 cupsminute rice
  • Sm pkgVelveeta cheese

Directions

  1. Melt butter, cook onion and broccoli till soft. Then put all ingredients in a bowl (cut up cheese) and mix. Put in casserole dish cook at 350 degrees 30 minutes. Do not cover casserole while cooking.
