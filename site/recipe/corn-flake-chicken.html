Corn Flake Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cupcrushed corn flakes
  • 1 6 oz envItalian dressing mix
  • 2 tbspgrated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tspparsley flakes
  • 6boneless skinless breasts
  • 1/4 cupbuttermilk

Directions

  1. Combine first 4 ingredients in a shallow dish. Dip breast in milk, roll on crumb mixture, place on a lightly greased baking dish. Bake at 375 degrees for about 18 minutes.
