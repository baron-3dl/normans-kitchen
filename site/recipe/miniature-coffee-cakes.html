Miniature Coffee Cakes

Photo: Bruce Guenter (CC BY 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1 cupflour tortillas
  • 3/4 tspbaking powder
  • 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 1/4 cupbutter
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1egg
  • 1/2 tspvanilla
  • 1/2 cupsour cream
  • 1/2 cupraisins (optional)
  • Topping
  • 3 tbspbrown sugar
  • 2 tbspgranulated sugar
  • 1/2 cupchopped nuts

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together. Cream butter and add sugar gradually. Beat till fluffy. Add egg and vanilla, beat well. Ads sour cream and flour alternately. Stir in raisins (?). Fill muffin tins (greased) 2/3 full. Add topping. Bake at 425 degrees 15-20 minutes.
  2. For topping add all topping ingredients together sprinkle on top, and bake.
