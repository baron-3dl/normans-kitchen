Dark Chocolate Sauce

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

A warm chocolate dessert sauce made from cocoa, sugar, corn syrup, and butter, served over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cupunsweetened cocoa
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 1/2 cuplight corn syrup
  • 1/4 cupvegetable oil
  • 1/4 cupbutter or margarine
  • 1/4 cupwater
  • 1 tspvanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine cocoa and sugar in medium saucepan; stir in corn syrup and oil.
  2. Cook sauce over low heat, stirring constantly, 8 to 10 minutes or until sugar dissolves. Add butter, 1/4 cup water, and vanilla extract; cook, stirring constantly, just until butter melts. Serve warm over ice cream. (Save for other uses.)

Goes well with

  • Bittersweet Topping for choc. cake
  • Broiled Bananas
