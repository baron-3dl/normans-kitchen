Chocolate Caramel Pecan Cheesecake

Photo: Lovin' Scoopful (CC BY 2.0)

A cheesecake built on a graham cracker crust layered with melted caramel and pecans, then topped with a chocolate cream cheese filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 14 oz.pkg caramels
  • 1 5 oz.evaporated milk
  • graham cracker crust
  • 2 8 oz.pkg cream cheese
  • 1/2 cupsugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tspvanilla
  • 3/4 cupsemi sweet chocolate chips melted
  • pecans, or walnuts, peanuts etc.

Directions

  1. Combine milk and caramel in pan and melt over low heat. Pour over graham cracker crust. Spread pecans evenly over caramel.
  2. Beat cream cheese at high speed till light and fluffy, gradually add sugar, mix well, add eggs one at a time, beating well. Stir in chocolate and vanilla. Beat until blended. Spoon over pecan layer. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven, run knife around edge of pan, cool on rack to room temperature. Remove from pan. Cover and chill for 8 hours. When ready to serve decorate with nuts, etc.
  1. Combine the evaporated milk and caramels in a pan and melt over low heat.
  2. Pour the caramel over the graham cracker crust. Spread the pecans evenly over the caramel.
  3. Beat the cream cheese at high speed until light and fluffy; gradually add the sugar and mix well.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla. Beat until blended.
  5. Spoon the filling over the pecan layer. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven, run a knife around the edge of the pan, and cool on a rack to room temperature. Remove from the pan.
  7. Cover and chill for 8 hours. When ready to serve, decorate with nuts.

Goes well with

  • Chocolate Cheesecake Pie
  • Chocolate Pecan Pie
  • Cheesecake
  • Chocolate Kahlua Pecan Pie
