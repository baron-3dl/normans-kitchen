Taco Salad

Photo: Maggie Hoffman (CC BY 2.0)

A layered taco-style salad with seasoned ground beef over a cream cheese and sour cream base, topped with tomato, lettuce, taco sauce, and shredded cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbhamburger
  • Taco seasoning mix
  • Taco sauce
  • 8 ozcream cheese, softened
  • 8 ozsour cream
  • 1 lgetomato, chopped
  • shredded lettuce, small amount
  • shredded cheese (about 1 small pkg)
  • onion, chopped
  • Nachos (1 lge box)

Directions

  1. Cook hamburg accorading to Taco seasoning directions. Let cool. Mix cream cheese and sour cream for first layer, then layer hamburg, tomato, lettuce, Taco sauce and cheese.
  1. Cook the hamburger according to the Taco seasoning mix directions. Let cool.
  2. Mix the softened cream cheese and sour cream together and spread as the first layer.
  3. Layer on the cooked hamburger, chopped tomato, shredded lettuce, Taco sauce, and shredded cheese.
