German Nut Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbmargarine, soft
  • 4 wholeeggs
  • 2 cupssugar
  • 1/2 pintsour cream
  • 1 tspflavoring
  • 2 cupsregular flour
  • 1/2 cupnuts
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 1/2 cupmaraschino cherries

Directions

  1. Mix together first 5 ingredients then add all other ingredients mixing well. Bake in a greased Tube pan for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
  2. Serve with whipped cream
