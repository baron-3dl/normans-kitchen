Chicken with Potato Crust

Ingredients

  • 1 largebaking potatoes
  • 3 tbspDijon mustard
  • 1 largeclove garlic crushed
  • 1/4 tspthyme
  • 4chicken breasts
  • non stick cooking spray
  • 1 1/2 tspveg oil
  • 1/4 tspsalt
  • 2 tspparsley or green onions

Directions

  1. Peel potato and shred thru grater to look like potato sticks. Transfer potato t bowl with ice water to soak for 5 min. Wash and dry chicken. Mix garlic, mustard, thyme in small bowl. Spread mixture evenly on top of each chicken breast. Fold thin end of breasts under to prevent over cooking., Coat baking dish with non stick spray. Arrange chicken mustard side up. Drain potaoes and blot dry. Mix potato with oil, salt and pepper, spread each breast with about 1/2 cup. Bake 30-35 minutes on upper rack. If it is not rown.Broil 3 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley or onions.
