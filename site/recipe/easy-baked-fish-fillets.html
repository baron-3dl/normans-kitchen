Easy Baked Fish Fillets

Ingredients

  • 2 tbspmayonnaise
  • 1/2 tspdried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tspsalt
  • 1/4 tsppepper
  • 1 lborange roughy, tilapia or other fish
  • 1/2 cupcornflakes crushed

Directions

  1. Mix first four ingredients. Brush over fillets. Dredge in cornflakes. Place on greased baking sheet. Bake at 450 degrees 10 minutes or till tender.
