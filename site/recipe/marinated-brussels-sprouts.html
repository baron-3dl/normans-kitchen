Marinated Brussels Sprouts

Photo: City Foodsters (CC BY 2.0)

A make-ahead vegetable side of frozen brussels sprouts chilled in an Italian-dressing marinade with onion, garlic, and dill weed.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pkg (10 oz)frozen brussels sprouts
  • 1 cupItalian salad dressing
  • 1 tbspfinely chopped onions
  • 1 clovegarlic, minced
  • 1/2 tspdill weed

Directions

  1. Cook Brussels sprouts according to directions; drain. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over sprouts and toss to coat. cover and refrigerate.
  1. Cook the brussels sprouts according to the package directions, then drain.
  2. Combine the remaining ingredients: the Italian salad dressing, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, and dill weed.
  3. Pour the marinade over the sprouts and toss to coat.
  4. Cover and refrigerate.
