Berry Easy Dump Cake

An easy layered dump cake made by spreading fruit pie filling, crushed pineapple, dry cake mix, butter, coconut, and chopped nuts in a pan and baking until set. Served warm.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13 by 9 by 2 in baking pan with butter or margarine. Spread the contents of 1 (21 oz) can of apple, blueberry, cherry, or strawberry pie filling evenly across he bottom of the prepared pan. Cover with the contents of 1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple (undrained). Spread 1 box yellow cake, white or lemon cake mix (yep, just dry _ right out of the box). Dot the top of the cake mix with pieces of butter cut from one stick. Sprinkle the top with 3/4 to 1 cup coconut and 3/4 to 1 cup chopped nuts of choice. Bake for 30 minutes. Serve warm.
  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13 by 9 by 2 inch baking pan with butter or margarine.
  2. Spread the contents of 1 (21 oz) can of apple, blueberry, cherry, or strawberry pie filling evenly across the bottom of the prepared pan.

  5. Dot the top of the cake mix with pieces of butter cut from one stick.
  6. Sprinkle the top with 3/4 to 1 cup coconut and 3/4 to 1 cup chopped nuts of choice.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes. Serve warm.
