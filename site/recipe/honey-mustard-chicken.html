Honey Mustard Chicken

Makes 4 servings

Photo: avlxyz (CC BY-SA 2.0)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupMiracle Whip Salad Dressing
  • 2 tbspDijon mustard
  • 1 tbsphoney
  • 4boneless/skinless breast halves

Directions

  1. Stir salad dressing, mustard and honey. Place chicken on grill or rack of broiler pan. Brush with 1/2 os sauce. Grill or broil for 8 to 10 minutes, turn and brush remaining sauce. Continue grilling or broiling 8 to 10 minutes or until tender. Makes 4 servings.
