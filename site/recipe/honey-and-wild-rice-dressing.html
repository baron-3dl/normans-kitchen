Honey and Wild Rice Dressing

A baked rice dressing combining cooked white and wild rice with sauteed onion, apple, and celery, plus walnuts, honey, parsley, thyme, and sage.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cupmargarine
  • 1 lgeonion, chopped
  • 2Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, chopped
  • 2 ribscelery, chopped
  • 1 cupwhite rice, cooked
  • 1/2 cupwild rice, cooked, drained
  • 1/2 cupchopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/3 cupchicken broth
  • 1/4 cupchopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cuphoney
  • 1/2 tspdried thyme
  • 1/4 tspdried sage

Directions

  1. Melt margarine in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, apples, and celery.
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Combine onion mixture and remaining ingredients in a large bowl.
  5. Transfer to a casserole dish and bake covered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
