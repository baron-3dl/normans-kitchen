Red Velvet Cake

Makes 2 layers, grease and flour pan

Ingredients

  • 1 cupcrisco
  • 1 1/2 cupssugar
  • 2eggs
  • 1 tbscocoa
  • 2 ozred food coloring
  • 1 tbsvanilla
  • 2 1/2 cupscake flour
  • 1 tbsvanilla
  • 1/2 tspbaking soda
  • 1 cupbuttermilk
  • 1 tbsvinegar

Directions

  1. Cream shortening and sugar, 2 eggs. Add cocoa and coloring in cup, add to . Sift flour and salt together, add buttermilk, folding. Add soda and vinegar in a cup. Fold in, add vanilla. Bake 20 to 30 minutes at 300 degrees. Makes 2 layers, grease and flour pan.
  2. FROSTING: Cream 2 sticks butter, 1 tsp vanilla, add 1 cup sugar. Whip until fluffy. Cook 1 cup water and 12 tbs of cornstarch until thick.
  3. Cool thoroughly and mix above and beat until looks like whipped cream.
