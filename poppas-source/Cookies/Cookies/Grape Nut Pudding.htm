Grape Nut Pudding
 
 
2   eggs, slightly beaten  
1/2 cup    sugar  
1/8 tsp    salt  
1/2 tsp    vanilla  
 
2 cups    milk, scalded  
2 tbs    grape nuts  
1 tbs    butter  
 
1 Combine eggs, sugar, salt and vanilla. Add milk gradually mixing thoroughly.
2 Pour into custard cups. Sprinkle grape nut on custard mixture in cups and dot with butter.
3 Place cups in pan of hot water and bake in moderate oven 375 degrees for 25 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Makes 6 servings.
4 NOTE butter maybe melted and combined with grape nuts before sprinkling on custard.
