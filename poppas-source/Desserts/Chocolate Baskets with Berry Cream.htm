Chocolate Baskets with Berry Cream
 
 
  Vegetable Cooking Spray  
1 6 oz. pkg    semisweet chocolate morsels  
3 2 oz.    squares chocolate flavored candy coating  
1/4 cup    strawberry preserves  
 
1 tbsp    orange flavored liqueur  
1 cup    whipping cream, whipped cream  
1 pint    fresh strawberries  
  Garnish: mint, strawberry fans  
 
1 Invert six 6-ounce custard cups; cover each with a paper baking cup then with heavy duty plastic wrap. Make sure paper cup stays in place. Place inverted cups on baking sheet. Coat with cooking spray.
2 Combine chocolate morsels and candy coating in a heavy saucepan; stir over low heat until melted. Remove from heat; let stand 10 minutes. Spoon into a decorating bag fitted with a small writing tip. (Or use a heavy duty zip top plastic bag, snipping a tiny hole in one corner.
3 Slowly drizzle chocolate over each cup. (If it drizzles too fast, let cool a little longer. If it becomes too firm to pipe, reheat it. Chill 10 minutes.
4 Pipe a second layer of chocolate; chill 1 hour. Carefully remove custard cups and paper cups from baskets, and gently peel away plastic wrap. Store in an airtight container in refrigerator until ready to serve.
5 Gently fold preserves and liqueur into whipped cream. Place chocolate baskets on plates; spoon cream mixture into cups. Arrange strawberries over cream mixture. Garnish, f desired; serve immediately. Yield 6 servings.
