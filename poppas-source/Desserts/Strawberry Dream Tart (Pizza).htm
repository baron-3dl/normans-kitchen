Strawberry Dream Tart (Pizza)
 
 
1 8 oz.    pkg cream cheese, softened  
1 14 oz.    can sweetened condensed milk  
1 5.1 oz    pkg vanilla instant pudding  
1/2 cup    water  
1 8 oz    container frozen whipped topping, thawed  
  Brown Sugar Crust  
1 qt    fresh strawberries, sliced  
 
  SUGAR CRUST  
4 cups    corn flakes cereal  
3/4 cup    butter or margarine, softened  
1/4 cup    firmly packed brown sugar  
1/4 tsp    salt  
1/4 tsp    almond extract  
1 1/3 cups    all purpose flour  
 
1 Beat cream cheese at medium speed with an electric mixer until smooth. Add next 3 ingredients; beat until smooth. Chill 5 minutes; fold in whipped topping.
2 Spread filling over Brown Sugar Crust; arrange strawberries over filling. Chill at lest 30 minutes before slicing. Yield: one 12-inch tart.
3 To Make Crust
4 Crush corn flakes; set aside. Beat butter and brown sugar at medium speed with an electric mixer until fluffy; add egg yolks, salt and almond extract, beating until smooth. Gradually add flour and cereal. Shape mixture into a ball; press into a well buttered 12-inch pizza pan. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely. Yield: one 12 inch crust.
